## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. What is the sunrise and sunset time for {city}, {state}, {country} today?

2. Are there any weather alerts or warnings currently in effect for {city}, {state}, {country}?

3. What's the average temperature for this time of year in {city}, {state}, {country}?

4. What's the chance of thunderstorms in {city}, {state}, {country} in the next 48 hours?

5. When is the next high tide and low tide in {city}, {state}, {country}?

6. What's the heat index for {city}, {state}, {country} today?

7. What are the current air quality and pollen levels in {city}, {state}, {country}?

8. How does the current weather in {city}, {state}, {country} compare to the historical average?

9. What's the 2-week weather outlook for {city}, {state}, {country}?

10. What's the current barometric pressure in {city}, {state}, {country}?
