## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Search for Tweets Containing a Quote: "Find tweets that mention the quote 'To be or not to be.'

2. Find Tweets about a Television Series: "Search for tweets about the TV show 'Game of Thrones.'

3. Search for Tweets from a Specific Date: "Find tweets about the US presidential election from November 3, 2020.

4. Find Tweets about a Specific Political Event: "Search for tweets about the COP26 climate conference.

5. Search for Tweets about a Book: "Find tweets about the book 'Sapiens' by Yuval Noah Harari.

6. Find Tweets about a Specific Technology: "Search for tweets about electric cars and the Tesla Model 3.

7. Find Tweets from a Specific Influencer: "Find the latest tweets from the popular fitness influencer Joe Wicks.

8. Search for Tweets about a Viral Challenge: "Search for tweets about the Ice Bucket Challenge.

9. Find Tweets about a Specific Music Album: "Search for tweets about Taylor Swift's album '1989.'

10. Search for Tweets about a Recipe or Food Trend: "Search for tweets about the popular Dalgona coffee trend.
