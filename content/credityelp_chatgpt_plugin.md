1. Calculate the repayment schedule for my personal loan of $8,000 at an interest rate of 3% for a term of 24 months.

2. What would be my repayment plan if I have a credit card balance of $3,500 and I want to pay it off in 12 months?

3. Calculate my personal loan repayment schedule if I borrow $50,000 at an interest rate of 6.5% for a term of 84 months.

4. What's the repayment plan for my credit card balance of $4,000 if I want to pay it off in 16 months?

5. Calculate the repayment schedule for my personal loan of $12,000 at an interest rate of 5.5% for a term of 48 months.
