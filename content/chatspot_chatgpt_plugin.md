## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Evaluate Company's Growth: "Use the ChatSpot plugin to discover the growth rate of {Company_Name} in the past few years."

2. Reveal Company's Market Share: "Can you use the ChatSpot plugin to find out the market share of {Company_Name} in its industry?"

3. Study Company's Products/Services: "Use the ChatSpot plugin to identify the core products and services of {Company_Name}."

4. Track Company's Stock Performance: "Can you use the ChatSpot plugin to find out the current stock price of {Company_Name}?"

5. Review Company's Partnerships: "Use the ChatSpot plugin to find information about the key partnerships of {Company_Name}."

6. Unearth Company's Target Audience: "Can you use the ChatSpot plugin to identify the target audience of {Company_Name}?"

7. Investigate Company's Marketing Strategies: "Use the ChatSpot plugin to find out about {Company_Name}'s major marketing strategies."

8. Inspect Company's Acquisitions: "Can you use the ChatSpot plugin to find out if {Company_Name} has made any recent acquisitions?"

9. Scrutinize Company's SWOT Analysis: "Use the ChatSpot plugin to perform a SWOT analysis of {Company_Name}."

10. Study Company's Corporate Culture: "Can you use the ChatSpot plugin to find insights about the corporate culture of {Company_Name}?"
