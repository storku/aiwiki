## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Find the top 10 highest transaction fees paid on the Bitcoin network in the last 7 days.

2. Compare the average transaction fees for Ethereum and Bitcoin over the last month.

3. What is the total supply of the Ethereum ERC-20 token {token_contract_address}?

4. How many new Ethereum addresses were created in the last 30 days?

5. Show me the transaction volume trend for Bitcoin over the last 3 months.

6. List the top 5 most used dApps on the Ethereum network in the last week.

7. What's the current Ethereum miner hash rate and block reward?

8. How many unconfirmed transactions are currently in the Bitcoin mempool?

9. Discover the top 10 most popular NFTs on the Ethereum blockchain in the last month.

10. Show me the number of Bitcoin transactions that have occurred on average per day in the last year.
