### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. What is the exact time in {timezone} right now?

2. Please give me the current time in {timezone}.

3. Can you show me the time in {timezone} at this moment?

4. What's the present time in {timezone}?

5. Tell me what time it is in {timezone} right now.

6. Give me the current time in {timezone}, please.

7. I'd like to see the current time for {timezone}.

8. What's the time in {timezone} at this instant?

9. Could you please provide the time in {timezone} right now?

10. Inform me of the current time in {timezone}.
