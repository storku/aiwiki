## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Reveal the guitar chord diagram for the <Chord_Name> in alternate tuning.

2. Show me the finger placements for the <Chord_Name> chord on the guitar neck.

3. Help me understand the fretboard positioning for the <Chord_Name> chord.

4. Demonstrate the proper finger positioning for the <Chord_Name> chord on a guitar.

5. Illustrate the guitar chord diagram for the <Chord_Name> with capo placement.

6. Explain the guitar fingering for the <Chord_Name> chord with a visual diagram.

7. Show me the chord shape for the <Chord_Name> chord on a guitar.

8. Display the fretboard diagram for the <Chord_Name> chord in DADGAD tuning.

9. Provide a guitar chord diagram for the <Chord_Name> chord in C tuning.

10. Show me how to play the <Chord_Name> chord on an 8-string guitar.
