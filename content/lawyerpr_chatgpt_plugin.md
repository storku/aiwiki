3. Find me a top-rated lawyer in [Sendai] who can assist with [tax law issues].

4. Recommend a lawyer in [Shizuoka] who specializes in [personal injury cases].

5. I need a lawyer fluent in [German] for [copyright issues] in [Hiroshima].

6. Who are the top 5 [environmental law] attorneys in [Niigata]?
