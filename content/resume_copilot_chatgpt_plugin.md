## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. How can I showcase my soft skills effectively on my resume?

2. I want to highlight my accomplishments in my previous jobs. What's the best approach?

3. How can I demonstrate my career progression within the same company on my resume?

4. What are the main aspects to focus on in my resume when applying for a remote position?

5. How can I incorporate industry-specific keywords into my resume for better ATS compatibility?

6. What type of format will make my resume more visually appealing while still looking professional?

7. Can you help me reorganize the sections in my resume to better emphasize my strengths?

8. What are the best practices for including references on my resume or in my application?

9. How can I showcase my freelance or gig work experience on my resume?

10. Can you help me write a powerful objective or summary statement for my resume?
