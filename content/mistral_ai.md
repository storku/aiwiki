Mistral AI** is a French [artificial intelligence](/wiki/artificial_intelligence) company headquartered in [Paris](/index.php?title=Paris&action=edit&redlink=1), [France](/index.php?title=France&action=edit&redlink=1). Founded in April 2023 by former researchers from [Google DeepMind](/wiki/google_deepmind) and [Meta Platforms](/index.php?title=Meta_Platforms&action=edit&redlink=1), [Arthur Mensch](/index.php?title=Arthur_Mensch&action=edit&redlink=1), [Guillaume Lample](/index.php?title=Guillaume_Lample&action=edit&redlink=1), and [Timothée Lacroix](/index.php?title=Timoth%C3%A9e_Lacroix&action=edit&redlink=1), the company develops [large language models](/wiki/large_language_model) (LLMs) and related tooling. The three co-founders originally met during their studies at [École Polytechnique](/index.php?title=%C3%89cole_Polytechnique&action=edit&redlink=1).[&#91;1&#93;](#cite_note-about-1) Mistral publishes both [open-source](/index.php?title=Open-source&action=edit&redlink=1)/open-weight models (for example Mistral 7B*, *Mixtral 8x7B*) and proprietary offerings delivered via API (for example *Mistral Large*, *Mistral Medium 3*, *Magistral Medium*).[&#91;1&#93;](#cite_note-about-1) In September 2025, it announced a €1.7 billion Series C led by [ASML](/index.php?title=ASML&action=edit&redlink=1) at an €11.7 billion post-money valuation, and reported a workforce of roughly 350 people across multiple offices.[&#91;2&#93;](#cite_note-reuters-seriesC-2)[&#91;3&#93;](#cite_note-lemonde-sept2025-3)

## History

Mistral AI was incorporated on April 28, 2023, by Mensch, Lample, and Lacroix, alumni of [École Polytechnique](/index.php?title=%C3%89cole_Polytechnique&action=edit&redlink=1) and veterans of [Google DeepMind](/wiki/google_deepmind) and [Meta AI](/wiki/meta_ai). Mensch spent nearly three years at Google DeepMind, while Lample and Lacroix are large-scale AI model specialists who had previously worked for Meta, with Lample being one of the creators of Meta's [LLaMA](/wiki/llama) language model.[&#91;1&#93;](#cite_note-about-1) The founders aimed to build AI models that are open, efficient, and capable of competing with those from larger U.S.-based companies.

In June 2023, one month after founding, the company raised a record €105 million seed round led by Lightspeed Venture Partners, including participation from investors such as [Eric Schmidt](/index.php?title=Eric_Schmidt&action=edit&redlink=1), [Xavier Niel](/index.php?title=Xavier_Niel&action=edit&redlink=1), and [JCDecaux Holding](/index.php?title=JCDecaux_Holding&action=edit&redlink=1).[&#91;4&#93;](#cite_note-reuters-seed-4)[&#91;5&#93;](#cite_note-ft-seed-5)

In September 2023, Mistral released its first open-weight model, **[Mistral 7B](/index.php?title=Mistral_7B&action=edit&redlink=1)**, under the [Apache License](/index.php?title=Apache_License&action=edit&redlink=1) 2.0, including distribution via a BitTorrent magnet link and later on [Hugging Face](/wiki/hugging_face). This release was notable for being distributed via a torrent file, which sparked some controversy regarding distribution methods but highlighted the model's accessibility.[&#91;6&#93;](#cite_note-mistral7b-6)[&#91;7&#93;](#cite_note-tc-mistral7b-7)

In December 2023, Mistral announced **[Mixtral 8x7B](/index.php?title=Mixtral_8x7B&action=edit&redlink=1)**, a sparse [mixture of experts](/wiki/mixture_of_experts) (SMoE) open-weight model.[&#91;8&#93;](#cite_note-mixtral8x7b-8) That month it also raised €385 million in a second round led by [Andreessen Horowitz](/index.php?title=Andreessen_Horowitz&action=edit&redlink=1) (a16z) and Lightspeed, bringing the company's valuation to approximately $2 billion. The round included investment from [Nvidia](/wiki/nvidia) and [Salesforce](/index.php?title=Salesforce&action=edit&redlink=1).[&#91;9&#93;](#cite_note-reuters-seriesA-9)

In February 2024, [Microsoft](/index.php?title=Microsoft&action=edit&redlink=1) and Mistral announced a partnership to offer Mistral's models via [Microsoft Azure](/index.php?title=Microsoft_Azure&action=edit&redlink=1), alongside a €15 million Microsoft investment structured to convert into equity in a subsequent round. The deal made Mistral's models available on the Azure cloud computing platform and established a research and development collaboration aimed at building applications for government and commercial customers. The arrangement drew review from [EU](/index.php?title=European_Commission&action=edit&redlink=1) competition authorities.[&#91;10&#93;](#cite_note-azure-mistral-10)[&#91;11&#93;](#cite_note-reuters-msft-11)[&#91;12&#93;](#cite_note-ars-msft-12)

In June 2024, Mistral raised €600 million at ~€5.8 billion valuation to scale compute and go-to-market.[&#91;13&#93;](#cite_note-ft-seriesB-13)[&#91;14&#93;](#cite_note-lemonde-seriesB-14)

On September 9, 2025, the company announced a €1.7 billion Series C led by [ASML](/index.php?title=ASML&action=edit&redlink=1), at €11.7 billion post-money; ASML became the largest shareholder (~11%).[&#91;15&#93;](#cite_note-mistral-seriesC-15)[&#91;2&#93;](#cite_note-reuters-seriesC-2)

### Models and APIs

Releases include open-weight and proprietary models geared toward cost-efficient inference, reasoning, coding assistance, embeddings, OCR, and audio input.[&#91;16&#93;](#cite_note-mistral-docs-16)[&#91;17&#93;](#cite_note-github-medium3-17)[&#91;18&#93;](#cite_note-infoq-medium3-18)

### La Plateforme

La Plateforme is Mistral AI's [API](/index.php?title=API&action=edit&redlink=1) service, which allows developers to integrate its commercial models into their own applications. The platform offers different pricing tiers based on model choice and usage, providing a scalable solution for businesses. Features include pay-per-token pricing model, fine-tuning capabilities, multiple deployment options (cloud, on-premises, edge), and support for both instruction-following and fill-in-the-middle code generation.[&#91;31&#93;](#cite_note-mistral-homepage-31)

### Le Chat

Mistral's assistant product **Le Chat** (pronounced /lə ʃa/ in French, meaning "the cat") is delivered as a web app and (since February 2025) on iOS and Android. Launched in February 2024, it provides users with direct access to Mistral's models. The company positions Le Chat for search, coding, vision, and enterprise workflows, with a Pro tier for advanced models and features. Key features include:

- Free tier with access to latest models

- Pro tier starting at $14.99 per month

- Team and Enterprise tiers for business use

- Multimodal capabilities (text and image processing)

- Document analysis and [OCR](/index.php?title=OCR&action=edit&redlink=1)

- Web search integration

- Memory system for persistent context across conversations

- Integration with 20+ enterprise platforms through [Model Context Protocol](/index.php?title=Model_Context_Protocol&action=edit&redlink=1) (MCP)

- Deep research mode for comprehensive analysis[&#91;24&#93;](#cite_note-mistral-chat-24)[&#91;25&#93;](#cite_note-lechat-new-25)

### Distribution and partnerships

Mistral distributes models through its own platform and via cloud partners, including [Microsoft Azure](/index.php?title=Microsoft_Azure&action=edit&redlink=1) (Azure AI model catalog), with **Mistral Large** first available on Azure in February 2024; the partnership attracted EU competition scrutiny.[&#91;10&#93;](#cite_note-azure-mistral-10)[&#91;11&#93;](#cite_note-reuters-msft-11)[&#91;12&#93;](#cite_note-ars-msft-12) Mistral Medium 3 is also published through partner marketplaces such as [GitHub](/index.php?title=GitHub&action=edit&redlink=1) Models and [AWS](/index.php?title=Amazon_Web_Services&action=edit&redlink=1)'s [Amazon SageMaker](/index.php?title=Amazon_SageMaker&action=edit&redlink=1).[&#91;17&#93;](#cite_note-github-medium3-17)[&#91;18&#93;](#cite_note-infoq-medium3-18)

## Philosophy and Strategy

Mistral AI's strategy is built on a dual approach:

1. **Fostering Open Source**: By releasing powerful models like Mistral 7B and Mixtral 8x7B under open licenses, the company aims to democratize access to AI technology, promote transparency, and prevent the technology from being controlled by a few large corporations. This approach also allows the community to build upon and audit the models, accelerating innovation and safety research.

2. **Commercial Excellence**: Through its proprietary models and API services, Mistral AI competes at the frontier of AI capabilities. The revenue generated from these commercial offerings funds the research and development required to create both open and closed models.

The company has emphasized computational efficiency in its model architecture, often achieving performance comparable to larger, more resource-intensive models from competitors. This focus on efficiency makes their models more accessible and cost-effective to deploy. The company's mission is to "put frontier AI in the hands of everyone" and democratize access to AI technology, with particular emphasis on European digital sovereignty, privacy-first approach with on-premises deployment options, and multilingual support (particularly European languages).[&#91;1&#93;](#cite_note-about-1)

## Technology

Mistral's open-weight releases emphasize efficient inference and permissive licensing (Apache-2.0). Notable innovations include:

### Technical Architecture

#### Sliding Window Attention

Mistral models utilize [sliding window attention](/index.php?title=Sliding_window_attention&action=edit&redlink=1) (SWA) mechanism where each layer attends to the previous 4,096 hidden states, enabling efficient processing of longer sequences while reducing memory requirements.[&#91;6&#93;](#cite_note-mistral7b-6)

#### Mixture of Experts

Mixtral models employ sparse mixture of experts architecture where a router network selects 2 out of 8 experts to process each token, enabling the model to have 56B total parameters while only using 13B active parameters per forward pass. This architecture balances quality and cost.[&#91;8&#93;](#cite_note-mixtral8x7b-8)

#### Mamba Architecture

Codestral Mamba uses the [Mamba2 architecture](/index.php?title=Mamba2_architecture&action=edit&redlink=1), a [state space model](/index.php?title=State_space_model&action=edit&redlink=1) offering linear time inference and the ability to model sequences of infinite length, particularly beneficial for code generation tasks.[&#91;22&#93;](#cite_note-codestral-mamba-22)

#### Reasoning Models

Later offerings (*Magistral*) focus on [reasoning](/index.php?title=Chain-of-thought_prompting&action=edit&redlink=1)-centric behavior with majority-vote decoding options.[&#91;28&#93;](#cite_note-magistral-28)

## Regulatory and policy context

Mistral's February 2024 investment and cloud partnership with [Microsoft](/index.php?title=Microsoft&action=edit&redlink=1) drew attention from the [European Commission](/index.php?title=European_Commission&action=edit&redlink=1) as part of broader reviews of Big Tech's AI alliances. Both companies stated they would cooperate with information requests. The UK's [Competition and Markets Authority](/index.php?title=Competition_and_Markets_Authority&action=edit&redlink=1) (CMA) cleared the partnership in May 2024, finding Microsoft did not have "material influence" over Mistral.[&#91;11&#93;](#cite_note-reuters-msft-11)[&#91;40&#93;](#cite_note-investopedia-40)[&#91;41&#93;](#cite_note-cnbc-cma-41)

## Controversies

Mistral AI has faced several controversies:

- **Microsoft Partnership Scrutiny**: The 2024 deal with Microsoft raised concerns in the EU about reinforcing U.S. tech dominance and deviating from open-source principles, as it involved a closed-source model.[&#91;11&#93;](#cite_note-reuters-msft-11)[&#91;42&#93;](#cite_note-sifted-42)

- **Adviser Conflict**: Former French digital minister Cédric O's role as an adviser sparked debates over potential conflicts of interest.[&#91;39&#93;](#cite_note-tc-overview-39)

- **Model Release Method**: The initial torrent distribution of Mistral 7B in 2023 was criticized for being unconventional.[&#91;43&#93;](#cite_note-medium-43)

Despite these issues, Mistral AI continues to emphasize its European roots and open approach to AI development.
