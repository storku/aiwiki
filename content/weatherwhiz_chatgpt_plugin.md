## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. What is the chance of rain in {city_name} today?

2. Can you tell me the expected high and low temperatures for {city_name} tomorrow?

3. What is the wind speed and direction in {city_name} currently?

4. Is there any severe weather expected in {city_name} in the next 72 hours?

5. Provide a 10-day weather forecast for {city_name}.

6. What's the humidity level in {city_name} right now?

7. I'm planning to visit {city_name} this weekend. Can you tell me the expected weather conditions?

8. How is the weather in {city_name} compared to {another_city_name} currently?

9. Are there any weather alerts or advisories for {city_name} right now?

10. I'm going hiking in {city_name} tomorrow morning. What's the weather forecast?
