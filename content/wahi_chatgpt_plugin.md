## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Search Property Listings near Public Transit: "Search for property listings in Toronto that are within {distance} of a subway station or bus stop.

2. Search Property Listings by Year Built: "Search for property listings in Toronto that were built between {start_year} and {end_year}.

3. Search Property Listings by Walk Score: "Search for property listings in Toronto with a walk score of {min_walk_score} or higher.

4. Search for Most Viewed Property Listings: "Search for the most viewed property listings in Toronto over the past {time_period}.

5. Search for Property Listings with Open Houses: "Search for property listings in Toronto with upcoming open houses.

6. Search Property Listings by Neighbourhood: "Search for property listings in the {neighbourhood} area of Toronto.

7. Search for Property Listings with Rental Suites: "Search for property listings in Toronto that have legal rental suites.

8. Search Pet-Friendly Property Listings: "Search for pet-friendly property listings in Toronto.

9. Search for Property Listings with Energy Efficiency Features: "Search for property listings in Toronto with energy-efficient features, such as solar panels or geothermal heating systems.

10. Search for Property Listings with Swimming Pools: "Search for property listings in Toronto that have a swimming pool.
