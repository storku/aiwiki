## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Find the current circulating supply of Binance Coin.

2. What's the all-time low price of Chainlink?

3. Show me the price change of Polkadot in the past month.

4. Find the top 5 cryptocurrencies with the highest 24-hour trading volume.

5. How much has the market cap of Uniswap grown in the last year?

6. Display the 10 cryptocurrencies with the largest price drop in the past week.

7. How does the 24-hour trading volume of Bitcoin Cash compare to that of Bitcoin?

8. What's the current price of Stellar in US dollars?

9. Provide the 7-day price chart for Solana.

10. List the top 3 gainers and top 3 losers in the cryptocurrency market today.
