## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. What is the latest analyst outlook on Apple Inc. (AAPL)?

2. Provide me the historical earnings call data for Microsoft (MSFT).

3. Can you show me the most recent insider trading activity for Tesla (TSLA)?

4. What are the key financial ratios for Goldman Sachs Group Inc. (GS)?

5. Display the last five years' sales growth for Amazon (AMZN).

6. Show me a discounted cash flow (DCF) analysis for Alphabet Inc. (GOOGL).

7. What is the current return on equity (ROE) for Coca Cola (KO)?

8. Provide an overview of the upcoming earnings calls for S&P 500 companies.

9. List the biotech stocks with the highest price-to-earnings (P/E) ratios.

10. What were the main points discussed in the latest earnings call for Netflix (NFLX)?
