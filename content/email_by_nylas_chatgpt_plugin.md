## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Retrieve the email attachments from the email with the ID {email_id}.

2. Show me email threads in my inbox with the label {label}.

3. Find all unread emails in my inbox from the past {number_of_days} days.

4. Search for emails with the keywords {keyword1} and {keyword2} in the subject.

5. Display email threads in my inbox where the subject contains {keyword} and has at least {number_of_replies} replies.

6. Move the email with the ID {email_id} to the folder {folder_name}.

7. List all emails from {email_address} that have been marked as important.

8. Show me the 3 longest email threads I currently have in my inbox.

9. Archive all emails older than {number_of_days} days in my inbox.

10. Create a new label {label_name} and apply it to all emails containing the keyword {keyword}.
