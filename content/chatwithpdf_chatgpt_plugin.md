## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Summarize the main points of this PDF document: [PDF URL]

2. What is the purpose of the study described in this PDF: [PDF URL]?

3. Describe the methodology used in the research paper found at this link: [PDF URL]

4. What are the key findings and conclusions from this report: [PDF URL]?

5. According to this PDF ([PDF URL]), what are the benefits of the proposed solution?

6. How does the author support their argument in this essay: [PDF URL]?

7. What are the main recommendations provided in this policy document: [PDF URL]?

8. Can you help me understand the statistical analysis used in this research paper: [PDF URL]?

9. Please provide an overview of the history discussed in this PDF: [PDF URL]

10. According to this PDF ([PDF URL]), what are the primary challenges faced by the case study subject?
