## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Find me a weekend getaway package from {origin} to {destination} including flights and accommodation, departing on {departure date} and returning on {return date}.

2. Search for luxury hotel deals in {city} with a spa and gym, checking in on {check-in date} and checking out on {check-out date}.

3. I need a compact rental car with unlimited mileage in {city} from {pick-up date} to {drop-off date}.

4. Explore affordable family vacation destinations within {destination_country} from {origin} with a budget of {budget in USD} for {number of people}.

5. Find me one-way flights from {origin} to {destination} in economy class with flexible dates between {start date} and {end date}.

6. I want to stay in a vacation rental in {city} with high-speed Wi-Fi and a fully equipped kitchen, checking in on {check-in date} and checking out on {check-out date}.

7. Search for red-eye flights from {origin} to {destination} on {departure date} with a layover in {layover city}.

8. Find me a hotel in {city} near {point of interest} with free airport shuttle service, checking in on {check-in date} and checking out on {check-out date}.

9. I need a luxury rental car in {city} for a wedding from {pick-up date} to {drop-off date}.

10. Explore budget-friendly honeymoon destinations from {origin} with a budget of {budget in USD} for a trip between {minimum days} and {maximum days}.
