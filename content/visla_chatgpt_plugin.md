## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Create a video illustrating the history and culture of ${country_name}.

2. Generate a video explaining the top 5 benefits of practicing yoga regularly.

3. Produce a short film about the life and works of ${famous_artist_name}.

4. Craft a video showcasing tips and tricks for effective time management.

5. Design a video on the best tourist destinations for solo travelers.

6. Make a video compilation of the most iconic moments in ${sport_name}.

7. Develop an inspiring video exploring breakthroughs in ${scientific_field}.

8. Create an animated explainer video about the evolution of ${technology_name}.

9. Generate a video for my podcast on mental health awareness and self-care tips.

10. Produce a video advertisement for my online course, ${course_name}.
