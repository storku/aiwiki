## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. What are some popular attractions to visit in {city} during my vacation?

2. Can you find me an eco-friendly hotel in {destination}?

3. I need a direct flight from {origin} to {destination} on {date}. Can you find options?

4. Can you suggest unique local restaurants to try in {city}?

5. What are my public transportation options in {city} for sightseeing?

6. I'm looking for top-rated bed and breakfasts in {destination}. Can you help?

7. What are the best cultural events or festivals in {city} during {month}?

8. Can you recommend budget-friendly accommodation options in {destination}?

9. I need information about trains from {city1} to {city2}. Can you help me with the schedule and pricing?

10. What are the best family-friendly beaches to visit in {destination}?
