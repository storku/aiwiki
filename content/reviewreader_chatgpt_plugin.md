## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Summarize the main features mentioned in customer reviews for this product: {product_url}.

2. Search for common complaints in Amazon reviews for this product: {product_url}.

3. Filter the top three rated {product_category} on Amazon by customer satisfaction.

4. Analyze Amazon reviews to find the most talked-about features of this product: {product_url}.

5. Compile the top five Amazon customer experiences for this product: {product_url}.

6. Find the most upvoted customer reviews for {product_name} on Amazon.

7. Discover the top three {product_category} for specific use-case "{use_case}" based on Amazon customer reviews.

8. Gather the overall sentiment of customer reviews for this product: {product_url}.

9. Identify the reasons why customers give five stars to this product: {product_url}.

10. Investigate recurring issues reported by customers in Amazon reviews for this product: {product_url}.
