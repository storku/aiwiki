## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. What are the trending news topics in the United States today?

2. Can you give me a quick summary of the top 3 national news headlines right now?

3. What are the most important news events happening in {city_name,state_name} today?

4. I want to know the latest news updates. Show me the top 5 national stories of the day.

5. Could you provide the key news highlights from {city_name,state_name} today?

6. Give me an update on today's top national news stories in the U.S.

7. What's new in {city_name,state_name}? Show me the top news highlights from the area.

8. Can you provide the top headlines from {city_name,state_name} today?

9. What are the most discussed news stories nationally at the moment?

10. Please update me with the latest local news from {city_name,state_name}.
