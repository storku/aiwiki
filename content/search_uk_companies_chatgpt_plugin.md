## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Search for UK registered companies with a specific SIC code from Companies House.

2. Find the 10 most recently incorporated companies in the UK from Companies House.

3. Fetch the PSC (Persons with Significant Control) data for a company with a specific company number from Companies House.

4. Retrieve the list of disqualified officers from Companies House.

5. Look up the incorporation date of a company with a specific company number from Companies House.

6. Fetch the last annual return date of a company with a specific company number from Companies House.

7. Retrieve the list of dissolved companies from Companies House.

8. Identify the accounting reference date for a company with a specific company number from Companies House.

9. Fetch the company status (active, dissolved, etc.) of a company with a specific company number from Companies House.

10. Retrieve the top 10 companies with the highest share capital from Companies House.
