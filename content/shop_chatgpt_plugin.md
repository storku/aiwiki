## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. I need a pair of wireless headphones with noise-cancellation features under {Max Price}

2. Find me a gaming console bundle within the price range of {Min Price} and {Max Price}

3. Search for a {Type of Product} made of sustainable materials under {Max Price}

4. I'm looking for a {Type of Product} with a {Color} pattern similar to this one: {Product ID}

5. Can you suggest some fitness equipment suitable for a home gym within the price range of {Min Price} and {Max Price}?

6. Search for a pet bed designed for a {Breed of Dog} under {Max Price}

7. I'm looking for a smartphone with a great camera and long battery life under {Max Price}

8. Find me a set of {Material} cookware within the price range of {Min Price} and {Max Price}

9. Can you suggest some board games suitable for a family game night under {Max Price}?

10. Search for a {Type of Product} that supports a charity or social cause under {Max Price}
