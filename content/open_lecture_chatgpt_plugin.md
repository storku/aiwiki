## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Find me open course lectures that focus on ${topic}.

2. Search for university lecture materials about ${topic}.

3. Can you locate university lectures related to ${topic}?

4. I'd like to find lectures that teach ${topic}. Can you assist?

5. Please find university course materials discussing ${topic}.

6. I want to learn ${topic}. Can you locate relevant lectures for me?

7. Discover university lecture transcripts or notes on ${topic}.

8. Help me access open course lectures that teach ${topic}.

9. Can you find any lectures that explain ${topic} in-depth?

10. Search for university lectures that provide a comprehensive understanding of ${topic}.
