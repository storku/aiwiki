## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Let's play a game of Crafty Clues, but with a twist: all clues must be given as historical events. Can you do that?

2. I want to play a game of Crafty Clues, but all clues must be given in the form of a famous movie quote without using character names. Can you do that?

3. Let's play a game of Crafty Clues, but with an additional restriction: all clues must be given as a palindrome phrase. Can you do that?

4. I want to play a game of Crafty Clues, but all clues must be given as a riddle involving mythical creatures. Can you do that?

5. Let's play a game of Crafty Clues, but with a twist: all clues must be provided in the format of a weather forecast. Can you do that?

6. I want to play a game of Crafty Clues, but all clues must be given as a tongue twister. Can you do that?

7. Let's play a game of Crafty Clues, but with an additional restriction: all clues must be given as a sports analogy. Can you do that?

8. I want to play a game of Crafty Clues, but all clues must be given in the style of a limerick. Can you do that?

9. Let's play a game of Crafty Clues, but with a twist: all clues must be given as a binary code sequence. Can you do that?

10. I want to play a game of Crafty Clues, but all clues must somehow incorporate the topic of outer space. Can you do that?
