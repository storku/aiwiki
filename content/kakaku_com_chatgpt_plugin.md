## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Compare prices for ${product1} and ${product2} from ${manufacturer}.

2. Show me the most energy-efficient ${product} from ${manufacturer}.

3. Search for ${product} with a ${specific_feature}.

4. Find the best ${product} for gaming under ${price} yen.

5. What are the top 3 ${product} with ${specific_feature} from ${manufacturer}?

6. Show me ${product} models with a warranty of ${warranty_period} years.

7. Find the best budget ${product} from ${manufacturer} in the ${category} category.

8. Search for ${product} with at least ${rating} stars within a price range of ${min_price} to ${max_price}.

9. Show me the ${product} with the longest battery life in ${category}.

10. What are the top 3 ${product} with free shipping under ${price} yen?
