## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Find clinical trials for disease_name that have been recently published or updated.

2. Search for clinical trials for disease_name that are in phase 1 or phase 2.

3. Provide a list of clinical trials for disease_name that involve alternative treatments.

4. What are the most notable clinical trials for disease_name with positive outcomes?

5. Find clinical trials for disease_name that include pediatric patients.

6. Search for clinical trials for disease_name that focus on prevention or early intervention.

7. What are the ongoing clinical trials for disease_name targeting rare or orphan diseases?

8. Find clinical trials for disease_name that are sponsored by pharmaceutical company_name.

9. What are the clinical trials for disease_name that are focused on non-pharmacological interventions?

10. Search for clinical trials for disease_name that are investigating the efficacy of combination therapies.
