## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Get the highest and lowest prices for the asset asset_symbol within the specified time range.

2. Compare the current prices of the assets in the list symbols_list and return their percentage change.

3. Get the market capitalization for the list of stock symbols stock_symbols_list.

4. Get the 24-hour trading volume for the list of crypto symbols crypto_symbols_list.

5. Retrieve the top 10 stocks by market cap within a specific sector sector_name.

6. Retrieve the top 10 cryptocurrencies by market cap.

7. Get the dividend yield for the list of stock symbols stock_symbols_list.

8. Get the daily candlestick chart data for the asset asset_symbol for the past 3 months.

9. Retrieve historical price data for the asset asset_symbol on a specific date.

10. Get the list of upcoming earnings reports for the stock symbols stock_symbols_list.
