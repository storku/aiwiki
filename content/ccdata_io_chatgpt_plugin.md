## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Get the current market cap ranking for ${crypto_currency}.

2. Show me the top 10 cryptocurrencies by market cap.

3. Provide the latest social media sentiment analysis for ${crypto_currency}.

4. Find the trading volume for ${crypto_currency} in the last 24 hours.

5. Get the total supply and circulating supply of ${crypto_currency}.

6. Show me a list of crypto market sectors with their market caps.

7. Find the top 5 exchange listings for ${crypto_currency}.

8. Get the latest crypto market overview, including total market cap and 24-hour trading volume.

9. Show me the price chart for ${crypto_currency} over the last ${days} days.

10. Find the list of top gainers and losers in the cryptocurrency market in the past 24 hours.
