## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Display the most recent trade data for the stock ticker {ticker}.

2. Retrieve historical forex data for the currency pair {currency_pair} within the specified date range.

3. Show me the top gainers and losers in the stock market over the past 24 hours.

4. What are the key financial ratios for the stock ticker {ticker}?

5. Find the estimated earnings dates for the stock ticker {ticker}.

6. Fetch the historical stock data for the ticker {ticker} with adjustable time intervals such as daily, weekly, or monthly.

7. Provide a list of recently added or delisted stocks on the NYSE or NASDAQ.

8. Retrieve the 52-week high and low for the stock ticker {ticker}.

9. List the market data for the top 10 cryptocurrencies by market cap.

10. Get the latest insider trading information for the stock ticker {ticker}.
