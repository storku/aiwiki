**Rentable Apartments** is a [Plugin](/wiki/plugin) for [ChatGPT](/wiki/chatgpt).              

## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Find me apartments in {city}, {state} with at least 2 bathrooms and a maximum rent of ${max_rent}.

2. Show me available furnished apartments in {city}, {state} with a minimum of 2 bedrooms.

3. What are the top 5 luxury apartments in {city}, {state} with rent below ${max_rent}?

4. Find apartments with underground parking in {city}, {state} that fit my ${max_rent} budget.

5. Provide a list of available apartments in {city}, {state} with wheelchair accessibility.

6. Show me the nearest apartments to {university_name} in {city}, {state} within-${max_rent} budget.

7. Find family-friendly apartments in {city}, {state} in close proximity to parks and schools.

8. What are the top 5 apartments in {city}, {state} with a walking score of at least 80?

9. Show me recently renovated apartments available for rent in {city}, {state}.

10. Provide a list of available apartments in {city}, {state} with in-unit laundry and central air conditioning.
