**HiCollectors Finder** is a [Plugin](/wiki/plugin) for [ChatGPT](/wiki/chatgpt).              

## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Find vintage [product] on eBay within the price range of [min_price] to [max_price].

2. Search for the top-rated [product] sellers on eBay with a maximum price of [max_price].

3. Provide a list of 5 rare [product] on eBay.

4. Find the best value used [product] on eBay, considering both price and quality.

5. Search for [product] on eBay with at least [number] bids and a maximum price of [max_price].

6. Provide a list of [product] on eBay that offer free shipping within the price range of [min_price] to [max_price].

7. Find a used [product] on eBay with a Buy It Now option.

8. Provide a list of 7 used [product] on eBay from top-rated sellers.

9. Find the latest auction listings for [product] on eBay with a starting price no higher than [max_price].

10. Search for [product] on eBay and sort the results by ending soonest.
