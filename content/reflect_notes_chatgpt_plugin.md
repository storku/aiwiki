## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Reflect on the primary lessons I learned from my recent project experience.

2. Analyze the key takeaways from the book I just read to improve my leadership skills.

3. Break down the experience of my last job interview and identify areas for improvement.

4. Explore the emotions I felt after completing a challenging task and what it taught me.

5. Think about the main insights after attending the recent personal development webinar.

6. Evaluate my performance in the latest team exercise and any areas where I could grow.

7. Create a reflection on my role in the recent group project, focusing on my contributions and areas of growth.

8. enerate a Reflect note on my thoughts after volunteering at the local charity event.

9. Reflect on the recent conflict resolution experience and describe the techniques used to navigate through the situation.

10. Contemplate my progress toward self-improvement goals and identify any habits that need further development.
