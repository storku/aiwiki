## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. What was the highest price for Bitcoin in the past 24 hours?

2. Provide the latest news regarding crypto exchanges and their performance.

3. Can you give me an overview of the market performance for the top 10 cryptocurrencies?

4. I want to know the latest updates about any security breaches or hacks in the cryptocurrency industry.

5. Show me the most recent news about mainstream companies accepting cryptocurrencies.

6. How has the total market capitalization for cryptocurrencies changed in the past week?

7. Find the latest news on established financial institutions getting involved with cryptocurrencies.

8. Can you provide the price change percentage for Ethereum in the last 24 hours?

9. Get me the latest news about innovative use-cases for blockchain technology.

10. Find any recent important events or announcements regarding cryptocurrency legislation.
