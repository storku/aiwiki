## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Can you log my breakfast? I had a yogurt parfait with granola and honey, and a cup of tea.

2. Please log my lunch. I had a veggie wrap with quinoa and a side salad.

3. I'd like to log my dinner. I had steak, baked potato, and a glass of red wine.

4. Log my afternoon snack. I had a bowl of mixed fruits and a few pieces of dark chocolate.

5. Can you add a food log for my after-dinner treat? I had two scoops of vanilla ice cream.

6. I had a green smoothie in the morning. Can you log it for me?

7. Please log my breakfast today. I had a ham and cheese omelet with a glass of orange juice.

8. Can you log my lunch? I had some sushi rolls, a cup of miso soup, and a side of edamame.

9. I want to log my dinner. I had chicken fajitas with guacamole, salsa, and a few tortilla chips.

10. Log my morning snack. I had a carrot cake protein bar and a cup of coffee.
