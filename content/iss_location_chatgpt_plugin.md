## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Can you tell me the current location of the International Space Station (ISS) and who is on board?

2. I'm curious about the ISS position right now and its crew members.

3. What are the current coordinates of the ISS and who are the astronauts in space?

4. Could you please inform me about the present location of the International Space Station and its occupants?

5. Track the ISS for me and provide the names of the team currently in space.

6. At this moment, where is the ISS located and which astronauts are on board?

7. What are the present coordinates of the International Space Station, and which crew members are in space?

8. Can you provide the current position of the ISS along with the names of the astronauts there?

9. Help me find the location of the ISS right now and the list of astronauts on the space expedition.

10. Where is the International Space Station at the moment, and who are the current crew members?
