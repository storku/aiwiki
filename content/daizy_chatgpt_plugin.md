## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Provide a technical analysis of the ${crypto_name} cryptocurrency over the last 30 days.

2. What are the top industries in the ${etf_ticker} ETF?

3. What factors could potentially impact the performance of ${stock_ticker} stock?

4. Summarize the latest research report on ${company_name}.

5. How do the dividend yields of ${stock_ticker1} and ${stock_ticker2} compare in the past year?

6. What are the key performance indicators for the ${asset_name} investment?

7. Determine the correlation between ${stock_ticker} and ${market_index} over the last 3 years.

8. Show me the five-year performance of ${crypto_name} cryptocurrency compared to the market average.

9. What's the expense ratio and net assets of the ${etf_ticker} ETF?

10. Provide a breakdown of ${company_name}'s social responsibility initiatives.
