## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. What are some strong SEO keywords for a site dedicated to [your_hobby_here]?

2. Suggest top-performing SEO keywords for a website about [your_profession_here].

3. Generate a list of SEO keywords for a fashion blog focusing on [your_style_here].

4. What are the most searched SEO keywords for a website about [your_pet_here] care?

5. Help me find SEO keywords for my new online course about [your_subject_here].

6. What are the optimal SEO keywords for a website offering [your_service_here]?

7. Suggest a set of SEO keywords for a health blog centered around [your_health_topic_here].

8. What are some relevant SEO keywords for a site about [your_educational_topic_here]?

9. Provide me with SEO keyword suggestions for creating content on [your_financial_topic_here].

10. Generate a list of powerful SEO keywords for a website on [your_environmental_issue_here].
