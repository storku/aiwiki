## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Identify the most effective PPC ads from {competitor_domain}.

2. Reveal the top-performing ads for the website {website_url}.

3. Please pull up the latest ad campaigns executed by {competitor's_domain}.

4. Help me uncover the recent PPC ads from {your_competitor's_website}.

5. Display the PPC ad data of the past month for {domain_name}.

6. May I see the latest ad performance metrics for {website_url}?

7. What are the key ad strategies implemented by {competitor's_domain}?

8. Share an overview of the newest ad campaigns from {your_competitor's_website}.

9. Get me the last three PPC ad campaigns for {domain_name}.

10. I want to investigate the latest ad trends from {website_url}.
