**Options Pro** is a [Plugin](/wiki/plugin) for [ChatGPT](/wiki/chatgpt).              

## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Calculate the option chain for the ticker {ticker} with expiration date {expirationDate}.

2. Determine the historical implied volatility for the option contract {optionTicker} for the past {days} days.

3. Find the top 5 stocks with the highest options volume today.

4. Display the open interest for the ticker {ticker} across various strike prices and expiration dates.

5. Provide the latest option statistics for the stock {ticker}, including volume, average trade size, and put/call ratio.

6. Analyze the impact of any upcoming dividends on the options for the ticker {ticker}.

7. Identify the most overpriced and underpriced options in the current market.

8. Get the 3-months forward volatility for the stock {ticker}.

9. Track the performance and returns of the option contracts for the ticker {ticker} since {date}.

10. Show me the Delta, Gamma, and Theta for the option contract {optionTicker} at different time intervals.
