### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Create a curriculum for learning digital marketing from scratch.

2. Build a beginner-friendly plan to learn app development in 6 months.

3. Design a comprehensive curriculum for understanding personal finance and investment.

4. Develop a step-by-step plan to learn professional photography for beginners.

5. Outline a curriculum for a 3-month intensive data analytics course.

6. Create a learning path to understand artificial intelligence and machine learning for non-technical individuals.

7. Design a curriculum for mastering sustainable living and eco-friendly practices.

8. Outline a course plan for learning musical instruments with a focus on the guitar.

9. Curate a curriculum for teaching graphic design skills to aspiring designers.

10. Develop a learning roadmap for understanding the basics of nutritional science and diet planning.
