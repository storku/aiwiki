## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Find me the latest LG OLED TV within the price range of $1000 to $2000.

2. Search for a black blazer from H&M under $100.

3. Can you look up the most popular K-Pop albums released this year?

4. I'm looking for a Nintendo Switch. Can you find it for me?

5. Search for a vegan skincare set within the price range of $50 to $100.

6. Can you find me a Swarovski necklace in the price range of $100 to $300?

7. I'm interested in FILA shoes for men. Can you find some options for me?

8. Search for the best air purifiers under $500 available in Korea.

9. Can you find me a Fitbit smartwatch within the price range of $150 to $250?

10. I'm looking for a Laneige beauty set under $100. Can you find it for me?
