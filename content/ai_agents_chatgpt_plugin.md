## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. I need guidance on how to develop a successful {specific type of app or software} from scratch.

2. Can you provide advice on how to improve my problem-solving skills for {specific type of challenges or industry}?

3. I need help with creating an effective budget plan for my {specific type of personal or business-related expenses}.

4. Can you provide guidance on how to improve my communication skills within a {specific type of team or professional environment}?

5. I need advice on how to manage stress and maintain work-life balance as a {specific profession or role}.

6. Can you provide advice on how to successfully pitch a product or service to {specific type of clients or customers}?

7. I need guidance on how to create an effective study plan for learning {specific subject or skill} in a limited time frame.

8. Can you provide advice on how to effectively manage conflicts in the workplace, specifically for {specific industry or team}?

9. I need help with optimizing my LinkedIn profile for better visibility in the {specific industry or job market}.

10. Can you provide guidance on how to develop and maintain a strong personal brand in the {specific industry or profession}?
