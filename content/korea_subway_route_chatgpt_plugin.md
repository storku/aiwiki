## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. How much time does it take to travel from {departure_station} to {destination_station} via the Seoul subway system?

2. What are the ticket prices for traveling from {departure_station} to {destination_station} on the Seoul subway system?

3. Identify wheelchair-accessible subway stations along the route from {departure_station} to {destination_station}.

4. Are there any tourist attractions near {destination_station} on the Seoul subway system?

5. What are the operating hours for the Seoul subway system during weekdays?

6. Find me the least crowded subway route from {departure_station} to {destination_station} during rush hour.

7. What subway lines connect {departure_station} to {destination_station} directly without any transfers?

8. List all the subway stations between {departure_station} and {destination_station} on the Seoul subway system.

9. How many transfers are needed to travel from {departure_station} to {destination_station} on the Seoul subway system?

10. What is the estimated travel time from {departure_station} to {destination_station} with the least number of transfers?
