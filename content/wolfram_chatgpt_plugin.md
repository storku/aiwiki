## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Find the limit of the function {function} as the variable {variable} approaches {value}.

2. What are the boiling and melting points of the element {element}?

3. What's the area and circumference of a circle with a radius of {radius} units?

4. Compute the series representation of the function {function} up to {number_of_terms} terms.

5. Find the roots of the polynomial {polynomial}.

6. What's the unemployment rate in {country} as of the latest data?

7. Calculate the mass of {element} in {quantity} moles.

8. What's the surface area and volume of a cylinder with a radius of {radius} units and a height of {height} units?

9. Find the fundamental frequency of a string with mass {mass}, length {length}, and tension {tension}.

10. What's the life expectancy in {country} as of the latest data?
