## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. What are the top sightseeing spots in {destination} for a photography enthusiast?

2. Can you recommend some romantic activities in {destination} for a couple's getaway?

3. I'm a nature lover. What are some outdoor activities in {destination} I should try?

4. Can you suggest some local festivals or events happening in {destination} during {month}?

5. What are some off-the-beaten-path experiences in {destination} for an adventurous traveler?

6. I'm interested in arts and crafts. Can you recommend some workshops or classes in {destination}?

7. What are the most popular local dishes in {destination}? Can you suggest some restaurants to try them?

8. Can you recommend some budget-friendly activities in {destination} for a group of friends?

9. What are some must-visit museums or galleries in {destination}?

10. I'm a wildlife enthusiast. Can you suggest some wildlife experiences or national parks in {destination}?
