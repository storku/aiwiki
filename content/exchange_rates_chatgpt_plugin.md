## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Compare the exchange rates of ${currency1} and ${currency2} against ${currency3}.

2. What is the percentage change in the exchange rate of ${currency1} against ${currency2} over the last week?

3. Which currency had the highest exchange rate against ${currency} in the last three months?

4. Find the exchange rate of ${currency1} against ${currency2} for the past ${number_of_days} days.

5. What was the three-month moving average of the exchange rate between ${currency1} and ${currency2}?

6. Calculate the total amount when converting ${amount} ${currency1} to ${currency2} considering a ${percentage} transaction fee.

7. Which top 5 currencies had the most significant upward or downward trend against ${currency} in the past month?

8. Display the real-time exchange rates of ${currency} against all major currencies (USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, and AUD).

9. What would be the exchange rate of ${currency1} to ${currency2} if it was converted using the ${specific_date} exchange rate?

10. Calculate the exchange rate of ${currency} against a custom currency with a fixed conversion rate of ${custom_rate}.
