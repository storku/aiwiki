## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Analyze the composition of this photograph.

2. What type of art style is evident in this painting?

3. Explain the emotions portrayed by the animals in this picture.

4. Describe the setting and atmosphere in this illustration.

5. What cultural elements can be observed in this image?

6. Identify the probable historical era depicted in this artwork.

7. What activities are taking place in this image?

8. Estimate the age of the tree in this photo based on its appearance.

9. Describe the dominant colors and mood in this picture.

10. What type of vehicle is represented in this image?
