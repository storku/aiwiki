3. I'd like to know the current value of Aave (AAVE) in Mexican Peso (MXN).

4. How much is 1 Algorand (ALGO) in Chinese Yuan (CNY)?

5. What's the current price of Cosmos (ATOM) in New Zealand Dollars (NZD)?
