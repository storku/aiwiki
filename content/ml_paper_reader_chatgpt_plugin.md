## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. I want to explore cutting-edge research on {keyword}. Can you find me some groundbreaking papers?

2. Help me find some highly-cited research papers on {keyword} for my research project.

3. I'm looking for comprehensive review papers on {keyword}. Can you find me some good ones?

4. I'd like to read some interdisciplinary research papers that combine {keyword1} and {keyword2}. Can you find some for me?

5. I need to find research papers that provide a historical perspective on {keyword}. Can you help me find them?

6. I'm interested in the practical applications of {keyword}. Can you find me some research papers that focus on real-world use cases?

7. I need to find research papers that propose innovative solutions in the field of {keyword}. Can you help me find them?

8. I'm trying to understand the limitations and challenges in {keyword} research. Can you find me some papers discussing these issues?

9. I'm looking for research papers that explore the ethical implications of {keyword}. Can you help me find them?

10. I need to find research papers that present case studies related to {keyword}. Can you help me find them?
