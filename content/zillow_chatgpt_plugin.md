## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Find me pet-friendly apartments for rent in {city}, {state} with a minimum of 2 bedrooms and a maximum monthly rent of {price}.

2. Search for waterfront homes for sale in {zip code} with a private dock.

3. Show me a list of {city}, {state} new construction homes currently on the market.

4. Provide a list of foreclosed properties for sale in {city}, {state} sorted by listing price from lowest to highest.

5. Find me a list of historic homes for sale in {neighborhood} built before 1950.

6. Show me top-rated elementary schools in {city}, {state} and their proximity to homes for sale.

7. Search for furnished houses for rent in {zip code} with at least 3 bedrooms.

8. Provide the median sale price of recently sold homes in {neighborhood} within the last six months.

9. Find me luxury penthouses for sale in {city}, {state} with rooftop decks.

10. Show me eco-friendly homes for sale in {zip code} that are fitted with solar panels and energy-efficient appliances.
