## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Can you provide a comparison of the top 3 {product_name} options based on price and customer reviews?

2. I want to find the latest offers on {product_name}. Can you show me the most recent deals?

3. Could you help me find the top-rated {product_name} within my budget?

4. I'm seeking the best value for money when it comes to {product_name}. Can you provide some options?

5. Could you find the most popular {product_name} based on customer ratings and current discounts?

6. I need your assistance in finding some limited-time offers for {product_name}. Can you provide a list?

7. I'm interested in purchasing {product_name}, can you provide the top 3 options with the lowest prices?

8. Could you help me find the best {product_name} with free shipping or any additional perks?

9. I'd like some assistance finding the latest promotions and deals for {product_name}. Can you help me with that?

10. I want to discover the top-sellers for {product_name}. Can you show me the most popular products according to customer reviews and sales?
