## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Recommend a Broadway show based on my interest in {genre}.

2. What are the upcoming Broadway shows in the next month?

3. Which Broadway shows have a strong female lead?

4. Provide a list of Broadway shows that are based on movies.

5. Find Broadway shows that have recently received rave reviews.

6. What are some popular Broadway shows with a comedic theme?

7. Which Broadway shows have the most elaborate staging and costume design?

8. Are there any Broadway shows with LGBTQ+ themes or characters?

9. Find Broadway shows that are written or directed by {name_of_writer_director}.

10. What are some popular Broadway shows with a historical setting?
