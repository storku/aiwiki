## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. I want to explore [your topic of interest] from a beginner's perspective. Can you suggest some introductory courses on edX for me?

2. What are the top-rated courses on edX for learning about [your topic of interest]?

3. I am looking for some free courses on edX to enhance my knowledge of [your desired subject]. Can you help me find them?

4. Are there any edX courses that can help me prepare for the [your desired exam or certification]?

5. I'm interested in programming languages. Can you recommend any edX courses that teach [your desired programming language]?

6. Can you help me find some edX courses that focus on practical applications of [your field of interest]?

7. I'd like to learn more about [your topic of interest] through hands-on projects. Can you recommend any edX courses with a project-based curriculum?

8. I have a limited amount of time. Can you find me a self-paced course on edX that covers [your topic of interest]?

9. I am seeking an edX course to help me with my career in [your field of interest]. Can you recommend one that offers a professional certificate?

10. What are some popular edX courses related to [your topic of interest] in the field of [your desired industry]?
