## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. What are the shipping options for the electronic component with the manufacturer part number {partNumber}?

2. Is there a volume discount available for the electronic component with the manufacturer part number {partNumber}?

3. What are the specifications for the electronic component with the manufacturer part number {partNumber}?

4. Are there any current promotions or deals on the electronic component with the manufacturer part number {partNumber}?

5. Provide a list of compatible parts for the electronic component with the manufacturer part number {partNumber}.

6. Which suppliers offer the best price for the electronic component with the manufacturer part number {partNumber}?

7. What payment methods are accepted for orders of the electronic component with the manufacturer part number {partNumber}?

8. Can the electronic component with the manufacturer part number {partNumber} be ordered as a sample or evaluation unit?

9. What warranty is offered on the electronic component with the manufacturer part number {partNumber}?

10. Are there any customer reviews or ratings for the electronic component with the manufacturer part number {partNumber}?
