## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Find articles that compare {technology1} and {technology2}.

2. Search for articles that provide an introduction to {framework_name} for beginners.

3. Find articles about the latest updates and features in {technology_name}.

4. Provide a list of the most well-received articles on {programming_language}.

5. Search for articles that discuss common problems and solutions in {topic_name}.

6. Find articles that focus on {concept_name} in {programming_language}.

7. Search for articles on the top {topic_name} tools and libraries.

8. Find articles that offer tips and tricks for optimizing {technology_name}.

9. Search for articles that explain the basics of {topic_name} for non-technical audiences.

10. Find articles that review the latest trends in {industry_name}.
