## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. What are the top 5 countries generating the highest amount of Internet traffic in the last 30 days?

2. What is the trend in the volume of DDoS attacks in the last 90 days?

3. What is the percentage of Internet traffic generated by browser type in the last 28 days?

4. How has the usage of Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) changed in the last 6 months?

5. What is the percentage distribution of traffic per Content Management System (CMS) used in {location} in the last 13 weeks?

6. What is the percentage distribution of different types of DDoS attacks observed in the last 3 months?

7. What are the top 5 categories of web threats detected in {location} in the last 14 days?

8. What is the percentage distribution of Attack Bundles detected by Cloudflare in the last 2 weeks?

9. Which Internet Service Providers (ISPs) have generated the most traffic in {location} in the last 24 hours?

10. What's the percentage distribution of DNS query types observed in the last 12 weeks?
