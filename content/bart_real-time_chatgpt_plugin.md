## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Provide me with the latest BART updates at the {station_name} station heading {direction}.

2. How long is the wait for the next train at the {station_name} station heading {direction}?

3. Fetch me the real-time BART updates for the {station_name} station heading {direction}.

4. What is the real-time status of BART service at the {station_name} station going {direction}?

5. When is the next BART train due at the {station_name} station heading in {direction} direction?

6. Get the current BART updates for {station_name} station moving towards {direction}.

7. Tell me the real-time updates of the BART at the {station_name} station heading {direction}.

8. What is the current BART service status for {station_name} station in the {direction} direction?

9. Find me the real-time BART updates for the {station_name} station going {direction}.

10. Access the current BART information for the {station_name} station traveling {direction}.
