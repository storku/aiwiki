## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. I need help finding a romantic gift for my partner for our anniversary. Any suggestions?

2. What's a unique and exciting gift idea for my best friend who loves traveling?

3. My mom is turning 50 soon. I would like to surprise her with an unforgettable experience. What do you recommend?

4. What are some interesting gift ideas for a teenager who is into video games and technology?

5. I want to buy a gift for my colleague who is passionate about the environment and sustainability. What do you suggest?

6. My brother just got a new job, and I want to celebrate with him. Can you help me find the perfect experience?

7. I'm looking for an educational yet fun gift for my young niece who's really into science. What should I consider?

8. My best friend is a huge foodie. Is there a unique culinary experience that would make a great birthday present?

9. My spouse is a history enthusiast. What kind of experience would you recommend as a fascinating gift?

10. Help me find the ideal experience for my fitness-loving dad's birthday. What are some options?
