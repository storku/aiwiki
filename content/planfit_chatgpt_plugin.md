3. Please suggest a 30-minute high-intensity interval training (HIIT) workout for a male weighing 72 kg at the beginner fitness level, to be done at home.

4. I'm a 68 kg female who is a fitness expert. I want a gym workout routine that focuses on my upper body strength and endurance.

5. Provide a workout routine for a 50 kg female at the intermediate fitness level seeking to work on her flexibility and balance at home.

8. Find a workout routine for a 62 kg female at the advanced fitness level wanting to work on her core and cardio primarily at home.

9. Create a workout routine designed for a beginner-level female weighing 58 kg who is looking to focus on toning her arms and shoulders. Gym workouts preferred.

10. Please design a workout routine for an expert-level male weighing 82 kg, focusing on plyometrics and functional exercises. I'll be doing this at the gym.
