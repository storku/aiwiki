## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Update my learning preferences for the topic {topic_name} with preferred teaching method of {teaching_method}, ideal pace of {pace}, difficulty level of {difficulty_level}, and preferred resources of {resources}.

2. Provide me with an overview of various learning styles and their advantages.

3. Offer guidance on how to set effective learning goals and objectives for the topic {topic_name}.

4. Illustrate the most efficient techniques for retaining information in the topic {topic_name}.

5. Could you please show me my learning preferences in all other subjects?

6. Suggest suitable learning materials and resources for the topic {topic_name} based on my learning preferences.

7. Provide me with a tutor's advice on staying motivated and focused during self-study sessions.

8. Alter my learning preferences for the topic {topic_name} to favor a more practical approach.

9. I'd like to know how a tutor can assess and adapt to different learning styles.

10. Help me create a personalized study plan for the topic {topic_name} based on my learning preferences.
