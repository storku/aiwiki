## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Retrieve the current METAR report for {airport_code} and compare it to the forecasted TAF report.

2. Fetch the TAF report for the next 6 hours for {airport_code}.

3. Provide a summary of the latest NOTAMs impacting {airport_code} focusing on runway closures.

4. Get the historical METAR reports for {airport_code} from the past week.

5. Retrieve the METAR, TAF, and NOTAM reports for {airport_code} along with a wind forecast for the next 12 hours.

6. Fetch the live aviation data and fuel prices for the following route: {airport_code_1}, {airport_code_2}, {airport_code_3}.

7. Provide a list of NOTAMs for {airport_code} with a focus on navigational aids outages.

8. Get the live aviation data for the nearest five airports to the following coordinates: {latitude}, {longitude}.

9. Retrieve the latest icing and turbulence forecasts for {airport_code} in the next 12 hours.

10. Fetch the aviation data that includes METAR, TAF, and NOTAMs for {airport_code} taking VFR conditions into account.
