**Hadith Advice** is a [Plugin](/wiki/plugin) for [ChatGPT](/wiki/chatgpt).              

## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Provide me with some hadiths that explain the significance of {specific topic or situation}.

2. What are the best hadiths for understanding {specific topic or situation}?

3. Can you share some enlightening hadiths about {specific topic or situation}?

4. I'd like to learn more about {specific topic or situation} through hadiths. Can you find a few for me?

5. What are some inspiring hadiths related to {specific topic or situation}?

6. Are there any hadiths that can help me make a decision about {specific topic or situation}?

7. I am seeking hadiths that offer wisdom on {specific topic or situation}. Can you provide some?

8. Can you find any hadiths that clarify misconceptions about {specific topic or situation}?

9. What are some hadiths that offer practical advice on {specific topic or situation}?

10. Are there any hadiths that encourage positive action in {specific topic or situation}?
