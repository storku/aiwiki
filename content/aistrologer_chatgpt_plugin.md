## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. What are the lucky numbers for ${zodiac_sign} on ${date}?

2. How does the planetary alignment affect ${zodiac_sign} on ${date}?

3. What's the travel horoscope for ${zodiac_sign} on ${date}?

4. What challenges does ${zodiac_sign} face on ${date}?

5. How can ${zodiac_sign} improve their relationships on ${date}?

6. Provide a family horoscope for ${zodiac_sign} on ${date}.

7. What's the compatibility between ${zodiac_sign1} and ${zodiac_sign2} on ${date}?

8. Give me the horoscope for ${zodiac_sign} for the upcoming month.

9. What's the emotional aspect of ${zodiac_sign}'s horoscope on ${date}?

10. How will ${zodiac_sign}'s day unfold on ${date}?
