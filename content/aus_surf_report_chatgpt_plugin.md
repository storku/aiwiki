## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. How's the surf at {location} in {state} this morning?

2. What can I expect from the surf conditions at {location} in {state} today?

3. What's the current surf status at {location} in {state}?

4. Can you give me an update on the surf at {location} in {state} today?

5. What's the wave quality at {location} in {state} today?

6. Are there any surf warnings or alerts for {location} in {state} today?

7. What's the current surf visibility at {location} in {state}?

8. What kind of waves can I expect at {location} in {state} today?

9. What's the best time to surf at {location} in {state} today?

10. How's the crowd at {location} in {state} for surfing today?
