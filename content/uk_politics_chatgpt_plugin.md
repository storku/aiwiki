## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Summarize the key points from the Prime Minister's most recent speech.

2. Find the political stance of <MP_Name> on LGBTQ+ rights.

3. Search for the voting records of <Party_Name> on environmental legislations.

4. Provide information about the major political parties participating in the next UK general election.

5. Find information on <MP_Name>'s contributions to local development in their constituency.

6. Search for the debate transcripts between <Party_Name_1> and <Party_Name_2> on housing policies.

7. Retrieve the recent statements made by <MP_Name> about the state of the UK economy.

8. Find <Party_Name>'s position on strengthening the National Health Service (NHS).

9. Provide information regarding any recent changes to the United Kingdom's voting system.

10. Search for press releases from <Party_Name> about their plan to address income inequality.
