## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. List the most recent lobbying activities in the pharmaceutical sector.

2. Could you provide information on recent proposed legislation related to environmental policies?

3. Find the recent congressional stock trades in the technology sector.

4. Can you provide data on the stock with ticker {ticker_symbol} and its lobbying activities in the last year?

5. Which public official or organization has been lobbied the most by company with ticker symbol {ticker_symbol}?

6. I'd like information on U.S. congressional members who have traded the most stocks in the past year.

7. Can you show me a list of recent congressional stock sales involving renewable energy companies?

8. Please provide summaries of proposed legislation in U.S. Congress related to cryptocurrency regulations.

9. What are the most influential lobbying organizations in the healthcare sector?

10. Are there any notable patterns in insider trading activities for the stock with ticker {ticker_symbol}?
