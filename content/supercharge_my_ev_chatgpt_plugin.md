## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Locate all electric vehicle (EV) charging stations within a {Distance} radius of {Location}.

2. Show me the nearest EV charging stations to {Location} that are open 24/7.

3. Which charging stations in {City} offer both level 2 and DC fast charging options?

4. Find charging stations in {City} that have {Amenities} available nearby.

5. I need a list of the top 5 highest-rated charging stations in {City}.

6. Show me all the EV charging stations in {City} that accept {Payment Method}.

7. Which charging stations in {State} are part of the {Network Name} network?

8. Find all charging stations along the {Highway Name} that accommodate non-Tesla vehicles.

9. How many charging stations are located at {Landmark}?

10. Which EV charging stations in {City} have {Minimum Power Output} kW or more capacity?
