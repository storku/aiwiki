## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Save my workout routine for 'Variable_Weekday' in my fitness tracker.

2. Retrieve all saved articles and notes related to 'Variable_Subject_Area' for my research.

3. Store the contact information for 'Variable_Person_Name' in my address book.

4. Find all saved events I have attended from 'Variable_Start_Date' to 'Variable_End_Date'.

5. Save the conference call details for the 'Variable_Team_Name' meeting on 'Variable_Date'.

6. Retrieve my saved preferences for 'Variable_Product_Category' when shopping online.

7. Store the details of my car service appointment for 'Variable_Car_Make' on 'Variable_Date'.

8. Find all messages in my 'Variable_Group_Chat_Name' where the keyword 'Variable_Keyword' was mentioned.

9. Save this to-do list for 'Variable_Task_Type' in my task manager.

10. Retrieve all invoices and receipts from 'Variable_Vendor_Name' for the past 'Variable_Time_Period'.
