## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. What is the lowest price for the ${DOMAIN_NAME} domain across different registrars?

2. Can you suggest available domains with hyphens related to ${KEYWORD}?

3. What are some available .co domains related to ${KEYWORD}?

4. What are some available .io domains related to ${KEYWORD}?

5. Which top-level domain options are available for domain names containing ${KEYWORD}?

6. Can you find available domain names that start with ${LETTER_OR_WORD} and end with ${LETTER_OR_WORD}?

7. What are some available domains that have ${NUMBER_OF_WORDS} words in them?

8. Show me a list of available domain names with the word ${KEYWORD} and less than ${NUMBER_OF_CHARACTERS} characters long.

9. What are some available alliterative domain names related to ${KEYWORD}?

10. Can you find available domain names that rhyme with ${WORD_OR_PHRASE}?
