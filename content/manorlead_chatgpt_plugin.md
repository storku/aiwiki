## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Find a 2-bedroom, 2-bathroom apartment for rent in {city_state} with a maximum budget of $1,500 per month.

2. Search for luxury homes for sale in {city_state} with at least 5 bedrooms, 4 bathrooms, and a budget of up to $2,000,000.

3. I'm looking for a 1-bedroom, 1-bathroom loft for lease in {city_state} with a maximum price of $2,000 per month.

4. Can you find a pet-friendly rental house for lease in {city_state} with at least 2 bedrooms, 1.5 bathrooms, and a fenced yard?

5. Search for condos for sale in {city_state} located near public transportation and with a maximum price of $400,000.

6. I need a furnished apartment for rent in {city_state} with 1 bedroom, 1 bathroom, and a maximum price of $1,800 per month.

7. Find me a duplex for sale in {city_state} with at least 2 bedrooms and 1 bathroom per unit, and a maximum price of $500,000.

8. Can you find senior-friendly properties for lease in {city_state} with at least 2 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, and a maximum price of $1,200 per month?

9. Search for an income property for sale in {city_state} with a minimum of 4 units, a garage, and a maximum price of $800,000.

10. I'm looking for a vacation rental in {city_state} with at least 2 bedrooms, 1 bathroom, and a beachfront view for weekend getaways.
