## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Find me a {property_type} in {location} with {bedrooms} bedrooms, {bathrooms} bathrooms, and a pool. My budget is {price}.

2. I'm looking for a {property_type} in {location} that has sea views and fits my budget of {price}. Can you help me find it?

3. I need a pet-friendly {property_type} in {location} with a minimum of {bedrooms} bedrooms. Can you help me find it?

4. I'm interested in a modern {property_type} in {location} with a spacious garden. Can you help me find it?

5. I'm looking for a {property_type} in {location} with easy access to public transportation. Can you help me find it?

6. I need a {property_type} in {location} within walking distance to the beach and with {bedrooms} bedrooms. Can you help me find it?

7. I'm interested in a {property_type} in {location} in a quiet neighborhood. Can you help me find it?

8. I'm looking for a {property_type} with a separate guest house in {location}. Can you help me find it?

9. I need a {property_type} in {location} near good schools and with a minimum of {bedrooms} bedrooms. Can you help me find it?

10. I'm interested in a fully furnished {property_type} in {location} for a short-term rental. Can you help me find it?
