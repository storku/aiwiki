## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Who scored the goals in the latest match between {team1} and {team2}?

2. Which teams played in the match with the highest-scoring game in the past 15 years?

3. What is the highest number of goals scored by {team1} in a single match within the past 15 years?

4. What is the winning streak record of {team1} in the past 15 years?

5. How many times has {team1} played against {team2} in the past 15 years and what is their head-to-head record?

6. List all matches in which {team1} scored 5 or more goals within the past 15 years.

7. Which teams participated in the highest-scoring draw match within the past 15 years?

8. Who are the top 3 goal scorers of {team1} during the past 15 years?

9. List all the soccer matches played on {specific_date} in the past 15 years, along with their scores.

10. What are the most memorable matches played by {team1} in the last 15 years?
