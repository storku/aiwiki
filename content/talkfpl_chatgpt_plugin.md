## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Compare the upcoming fixture difficulty for variable: team 1 and variable: team 2 in FPL.

2. Show me the highest-scoring budget players in the current FPL season.

3. Provide me with the latest information regarding variable: player's price changes in FPL.

4. Show me the best captaincy options for the upcoming gameweek in FPL.

5. Provide me with suggestions for wildcard changes in my FPL team.

6. Show me the most popular formation choices among FPL managers in the current season.

7. Provide me with set-piece takers for variable: team in FPL.

8. Show me the top scoring FPL defenders in the current season.

9. Provide me with a list of in-form players for the last 4 gameweeks in FPL.

10. Show me the season history of variable: player in FPL.
