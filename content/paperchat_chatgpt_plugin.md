**PaperChat** is a [Plugin](/wiki/plugin) for [ChatGPT](/wiki/chatgpt).              

## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Find the top 5 papers in the last year on [your_topic] published on arXiv.

2. Identify recent papers on arXiv that present breakthroughs in [your_field_of_study].

3. Search for papers that focus on the ethical implications of [your_topic] on arXiv.

4. Retrieve case studies or examples related to [your_topic] from arXiv publications.

5. Find papers on arXiv that compare different methods or approaches for [your_problem_statement].

6. What are the latest papers discussing experimental results in [your_field_of_study] on arXiv?

7. Search for recent review articles on [your_topic] published on arXiv.

8. Find the latest publications on arXiv that propose innovative ideas in [your_field_of_study].

9. Retrieve the most downloaded papers of the last month in [your_field_of_study] from arXiv.

10. Find arXiv publications that focus on the practical applications of [your_topic] in real-world scenarios.
