## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Reveal the appearance of website_url on YYYYMMDDHHMMSS in the past.

2. Display a snapshot of website_url as it was on YYYYMMDDHHMMSS.

3. Let me see how website_url appeared on YYYYMMDDHHMMSS in the past.

4. I'd like to view a version of website_url from YYYYMMDDHHMMSS. Can you show me?

5. Could you display how website_url looked on the date YYYYMMDDHHMMSS?

6. Take me to the past and show website_url's design on YYYYMMDDHHMMSS.

7. Show me the website_url's appearance back on YYYYMMDDHHMMSS.

8. I'm interested in how website_url was presented on YYYYMMDDHHMMSS.

9. Present a historical image of website_url at YYYYMMDDHHMMSS.

10. Display the visual history of website_url as of YYYYMMDDHHMMSS.
