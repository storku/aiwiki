**Video Insights** is a [Plugin](/wiki/plugin) for [ChatGPT](/wiki/chatgpt).              

## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Recommend Similar YouTube Videos: "Based on the content and style of the YouTube video with this ID: {YouTube Video ID}, recommend me 5 similar videos.

2. Analyze Video Thumbnail: "Analyze the thumbnail of the YouTube video with this ID: {YouTube Video ID} and provide insights on its visual appeal, relevance, and quality.

3. Analyze Video Tags: "Analyze the tags of the YouTube video with this ID: {YouTube Video ID} and suggest improvements for better discoverability.

4. Analyze Video Description: "Analyze the description of the YouTube video with this ID: {YouTube Video ID} and suggest changes for better clarity, relevance, and SEO optimization.

5. Discover Trending Videos: "Give me a list of the top 10 trending videos on YouTube in the {Category} category from the past 24 hours.

6. Identify Collaborations: "Identify the collaborations or crossovers in the YouTube video with this ID: {YouTube Video ID} and provide the names of the channels involved.

7. Retrieve Video Credits: "Retrieve all available credits from the YouTube video with this ID: {YouTube Video ID}, including the cast, crew, music, and production details.

8. Analyze Video Comments: "Analyze the comments on the YouTube video with this ID: {YouTube Video ID} and provide a summary of their sentiment and recurring themes.

9. Analyze a Video Playlist: "Analyze this YouTube playlist: {Playlist URL/ID} and provide a brief summary of the overall content and recurring themes.

10. Discover Channel Growth Factors: "Analyze the YouTube channel with this ID: {Channel ID} and provide insights into the factors contributing to its growth, including video content, engagement, and audience demographics.
