## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Analyze Meta Tags: "Use the SEOAnalysis plugin to analyze the meta tags of the webpage at {URL}.

2. Assess Heading Structure: "Can you use the SEOAnalysis plugin to assess the heading structure of the webpage at {URL}?

3. Evaluate Image Optimization: "Use the SEOAnalysis plugin to evaluate the image optimization of the webpage at {URL}.

4. Determine Organic Click-Through Rates: "Can you use the SEOAnalysis plugin to determine the organic click-through rates for the webpage at {URL}?

5. Review Anchor Text Distribution: "Use the SEOAnalysis plugin to review the anchor text distribution of the backlinks on the webpage at {URL}.

6. Analyze Competitor Keyword Strategy: "Can you use the SEOAnalysis plugin to analyze the keyword strategy of competitor at {CompetitorURL}?

7. Check URL Structure: "Use the SEOAnalysis plugin to check the URL structure of the webpage at {URL}.

8. Determine Quality and Relevance of Content: "Can you use the SEOAnalysis plugin to determine the quality and relevance of content on the webpage at {URL}?

9. Identify Duplicate Content Issues: "Use the SEOAnalysis plugin to identify duplicate content issues on the webpage at {URL}.

10. Evaluate Technical SEO Issues: "Can you use the SEOAnalysis plugin to evaluate potential technical SEO issues on the webpage at {URL}?
