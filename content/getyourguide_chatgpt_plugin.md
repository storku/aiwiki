## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Find me a bike tour in ${location} that costs less than ${price_lte} dollars.

2. I'm looking for a hot air balloon ride in ${location} that is under ${price_lte} dollars.

3. Can you recommend a snorkeling or scuba diving experience in ${location} that costs between ${price_gte} and ${price_lte} dollars?

4. What are some exciting hiking and adventure tours in ${location} that are under ${price_lte} dollars?

5. Find me a kid-friendly activity in ${location} that costs less than ${price_lte} dollars.

6. I'm interested in a painting or art workshop in ${location} that is under ${price_lte} dollars.

7. Can you suggest a local market tour in ${location} that costs between ${price_gte} and ${price_lte} dollars?

8. What are some sightseeing cruise options in ${location} that are under ${price_lte} dollars?

9. Find me a ghost or mystery tour in ${location} that costs less than ${price_lte} dollars.

10. I'm looking for a wellness or spa experience in ${location} that is under ${price_lte} dollars.
