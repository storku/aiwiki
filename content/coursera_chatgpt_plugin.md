## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Discover popular courses in {subject_name} on Coursera.

2. Find short-duration courses in {subject_name} on Coursera.

3. Search for Coursera courses with a focus on {industry_name}.

4. Identify highly-rated courses in {subject_name} on Coursera.

5. Look for Coursera courses that offer professional certificates in {field_name}.

6. Find language learning courses on Coursera.

7. Search for courses that teach {specific_skill} on Coursera.

8. Discover top specializations in {subject_name} on Coursera.

9. Identify courses with hands-on projects in {field_name} on Coursera.

10. Find free courses available on Coursera in {subject_name}.
