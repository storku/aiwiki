## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Create a customized email to approach the job candidate at {profile_link}, for the job position at {job_description_link}.

2. Design a professional outreach email for the potential employee at {profile_link}, considering them for the role at {job_description_link}.

3. Compose a captivating email to connect with the candidate at {profile_link}, for the job opportunity described at {job_description_link}.

4. Draft a warm and personalized email for the job seeker at {profile_link}, for the role mentioned at {job_description_link}.

5. Produce a motivating recruitment email for the candidate with a profile at {profile_link}, for the job role at {job_description_link}.

6. Generate an impactful email to the potential hire at {profile_link}, in regards to the job opportunity at {job_description_link}.

7. Formulate an attention-grabbing email for the job candidate at {profile_link}, for the position detailed at {job_description_link}.

8. Assemble a tailored outreach email for the candidate at {profile_link}, showcasing their fit for the role at {job_description_link}.

9. Create a noteworthy email to approach the job seeker at {profile_link}, for the position mentioned in the job post at {job_description_link}.

10. Invent a sincere and engaging email for the prospective employee found at {profile_link}, for the job opportunity at {job_description_link}.
