## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Find me a luxury car rental in {city} from {start_date} to {end_date} with unlimited mileage.

2. Can you show me the options for minibus rentals in {city} for a trip starting on {start_date} and ending on {end_date}?

3. I need a compact car with GPS included for my trip from {pickup_location} to {dropoff_location} on {date}. Can you help me find one?

4. I'm looking for a car rental with automatic transmission in {city} from {start_date} to {end_date}. Can you help me find one?

5. Find me a car rental in {city} from {start_date} to {end_date} that allows additional drivers.

6. Can you show me the options for luxury sports car rentals in {city} for the next weekend?

7. I'm planning a road trip from {pickup_location} to {dropoff_location} on {date} and I need a pet-friendly car rental. Can you help me find one?

8. I need a hybrid car for rent in {city} from {start_date} to {end_date}. Can you help me find one?

9. Find me a car rental from {pickup_location} to {dropoff_location} on {date} that includes insurance coverage.

10. I'm looking for an all-wheel-drive (AWD) car rental in {city} from {start_date} to {end_date}. Can you help me find one?
