7. "Preferred Marketing Strategies/Frameworks:" with options a) Choose for me, b) Others (specify).

8. "Revise and Review Generated Ad Draft. Provide your thoughts on the generated ad copy and any desired revisions." considering the platform's word limit, for example 280 characters for Twitter/X.com.
