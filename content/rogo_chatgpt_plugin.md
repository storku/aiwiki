## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Find the top 5 trending open-source repositories on GitHub in the {category} category.

2. What are the most popular open-source projects related to machine learning on GitHub?

3. Give me a list of the 5 most recently created open-source repositories with over 100 stars.

4. Which open-source repositories have the highest number of downloads in the past 3 months?

5. How many contributors does the {repo} repository have and what is the percentage of open issues?

6. List the top 5 open-source repositories with the most extensive documentation.

7. Show me the top open-source projects in the {domain} domain with a focus on performance.

8. What are the most popular repositories in the {language} language that have adopted the {license} license?

9. Provide a list of repositories that have integrated continuous integration (CI) tools in the past year.

10. Which open-source projects have the most positive sentiment in their GitHub issues and pull requests comments?
