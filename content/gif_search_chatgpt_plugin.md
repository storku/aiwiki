**GIF Search** is a [Plugin](/wiki/plugin) for [ChatGPT](/wiki/chatgpt).              

## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Gifs for TV Shows: "Find a gif from the TV show {tv_show_name}.

2. Gifs for Virtual Hugs: "Show me a gif that represents a virtual hug.

3. Gifs for Congratulating: "Find a congratulations gif for a graduation.

4. Gifs of Food Items: "Show me a gif of delicious {food_item_name}.

5. Gifs for Encouragement: "Find a gif that shows support and cheering.

6. Gifs for Dance Moves: "Show me a gif of the {dance_move_name} dance move.

7. Gifs for Responses: "Find a gif that expresses disbelief.

8. Gifs for Nature and Scenery: "Show me a gif of a beautiful sunset.

9. Gifs for Love and Relationships: "Find a romantic gif for my partner.

10. Gifs for Travel: "Show me a gif of the {destination_name} tourist attraction.
