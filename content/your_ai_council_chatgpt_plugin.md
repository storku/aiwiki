## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. I would appreciate the AI council's input on question.

2. Could you request the AI council's views on question?

3. Please gather the AI council's thoughts on question.

4. I'm interested in the AI council's analysis of question.

5. Seek the AI council's expert opinions on question.

6. Can you inquire the AI council's stance on question?

7. I'm looking for the AI council's diverse insights on question.

8. Please ask the AI council for their take on question.

9. Obtain the AI council's viewpoints on question, please.

10. Request the AI council's perspective on question.
