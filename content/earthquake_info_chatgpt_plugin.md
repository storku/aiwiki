## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Which countries experienced an earthquake in the past week?

2. What is the average magnitude of earthquakes in the last 30 days?

3. Have there been any tsunami warnings issued due to recent earthquakes?

4. How many earthquakes with a magnitude higher than 5 have occurred this month?

5. What was the most common depth for earthquakes in the past week?

6. What was the highest magnitude earthquake in the last 6 months?

7. What is the average frequency of earthquakes in the last year?

8. Which tectonic plates were involved in the most recent earthquake?

9. Were there any reported casualties from the most recent earthquake?

10. What are the primary regions affected by earthquakes in the past 3 months?
