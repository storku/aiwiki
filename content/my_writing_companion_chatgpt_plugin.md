## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Looking for a writer with expertise in {writing_category} for my content needs. Can you assist?

2. I require a skilled writer with experience in the {writing_category} industry. Help me find the right one.

3. Help me find a writer who has a solid track record of creating content in the {writing_category} sector.

4. Please find me a writer who is well-versed in {writing_category} for my upcoming project.

5. I need a writer who excels in {writing_category} to deliver outstanding content. Can you find one for me?

6. Assist me in finding a professional writer who specializes in {writing_category} to create top-notch content.

7. Can you recommend a writer with an impressive portfolio in the {writing_category} niche?

8. I'm seeking a writer with extensive knowledge in {writing_category} for my content needs. Can you find one?

9. Help me locate a writer with exceptional skills in {writing_category} to craft engaging content.

10. I need an experienced writer who is highly skilled in {writing_category} for my project. Can you help me find one?
