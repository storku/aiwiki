## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Display the most recent Mars Rover photos taken.

2. Find images related to the International Space Station.

3. Show me pictures of the Horsehead Nebula.

4. Retrieve images of the Milky Way as seen from Earth.

5. Find images of famous comets, like Halley's Comet or Comet NEOWISE.

6. Display the Astronomy Picture of the Day for the last summer solstice.

7. Search for images of Jupiter and its moons.

8. Show me spectacular images of the Northern Lights from space.

9. Retrieve Mars Rover photos featuring different Martian rock formations.

10. Find images related to the Space Shuttle program.
