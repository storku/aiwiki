## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Can you provide the top 3 trending topics related to health and wellness in Brazil?

2. What are the trending travel destinations in the United States this week?

3. What are the top 5 most talked-about movies on Twitter in the United Kingdom today?

4. Find the most popular tweets discussing the latest Apple product release.

5. What are the current trending food topics in Japan in the Japanese language?

6. Search for tweets about the ongoing environmental issues in the European Union.

7. What are the latest trends in the cryptocurrency market in India?

8. Discover the trending hashtags related to mental health awareness in Canada.

9. Show me the recent popular discussions on Twitter about sustainable living practices.

10. Find the top trending topics in sports in Spain today.
