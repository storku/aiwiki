## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Find me startups in {Location} that use {Specific_Technology} in their products or services and are within my budget of {Max_Price}

2. Show me startups that have been recently added for sale in {Location} within the price range of {Min_Price} to {Max_Price}

3. Search for profitable startups in the {Startup_Type} sector with a minimum annual profit of {Min_Profit}

4. I am interested in startups with at least {Min_Employees} employees. Can you find some options in {Location} within my budget of {Max_Price}?

5. Search for startups having a growth rate of at least {Min_Growth_Rate}% in the {Startup_Type} sector within a price range of {Min_Price} to {Max_Price}

6. Can you show me startups in {Location} that have received funding from {Investor_Name} and are for sale?

7. Find me startups for sale founded by {Specific_Founder} within my budget of {Max_Price}

8. I am interested in startups that have partnerships with {Partner_Name}. Can you find some options within a price range of {Min_Price} to {Max_Price}?

9. Show me startups in {Location} with at least {Min_Customer_Count} customers and within my budget of {Max_Price}

10. Search for exits of startups in {Location} within the last {Time_Period} and a price range of {Min_Price} to {Max_Price}
