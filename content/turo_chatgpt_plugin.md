## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Search for a convertible car available for rent in {location} from {start_date} to {end_date}.

2. Find a car with {fuel_type} fuel type available for rent in {location} from {start_date} to {end_date}.

3. Look for a luxury car available for rent in {location} from {start_date} to {end_date}.

4. Find an eco-friendly car with {min_mpg} minimum MPG available for rent in {location} from {start_date} to {end_date}.

5. Search for a car that allows pets in {location} from {start_date} to {end_date}.

6. Find a car with bike rack availability for rent in {location} from {start_date} to {end_date}.

7. Search for a car with delivery option to {delivery_location} available for rent in {location} from {start_date} to {end_date}.

8. Find a car with a sunroof or moonroof available for rent in {location} from {start_date} to {end_date}.

9. Search for a car with unlimited mileage offer available for rent in {location} from {start_date} to {end_date}.

10. Find a car with an {all_wheel_drive} drivetrain available for rent in {location} from {start_date} to {end_date}.
