## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Find the latest news articles on ${topic} published within the last ${timeframe}.

2. Provide the abstract and conclusion of the Arxiv.org paper at ${arxiv_uri}.

3. Search for global news related to ${event} occurring in ${region}.

4. Retrieve a list of the latest Arxiv.org research papers in the field of ${discipline}.

5. Find news articles from ${publisher} on the topic of ${subject} within the past ${timeframe}.

6. Get the most trending news articles in ${category} for the past ${time_period}.

7. Compare Arxiv.org research papers on ${topic_1} and ${topic_2} in terms of ${comparison_criteria}.

8. Search for Arxiv.org research papers co-authored by ${author_1} and ${author_2} on ${topic}.

9. Find the latest news articles on ${topic} and their potential impact on ${industry}.

10. Summarize the key findings from multiple Arxiv.org research papers related to ${topic}.
