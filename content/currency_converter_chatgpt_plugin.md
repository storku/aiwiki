## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. I'm an art collector and I want to buy a painting from France. Can you convert $3500 to Euros for me?

2. I'm a musician and I have a gig in Mexico. How much would my performance fee of $600 be in Mexican Pesos?

3. I'm a fashion designer and I want to source fabric from Brazil. Can you convert $750 to Brazilian Reais for me?

4. I'm a cryptocurrency trader and I want to cash out some profits in South Korean Won. How much would $5000 be in KRW?

5. I'm an online seller and I have just sold items on an international platform. Can you convert 2500 Swedish Krona to US dollars for me?

6. I'm a graphic designer working remotely from New Zealand. How much would my salary of $3000 be in New Zealand dollars?

7. I'm a language teacher and I have a student from Russia. Can you convert my lesson fee of $40 to Russian Rubles for me?

8. I'm a tourist planning to explore Egypt. How much would my budget of $1000 be in Egyptian Pounds?

9. I'm an expat living in Argentina. Can you convert my salary of $50,000 to Argentine Pesos for me?

10. I'm a worker remitting money to my family in the Philippines. Can you convert $300 to Philippine Pesos for me?
