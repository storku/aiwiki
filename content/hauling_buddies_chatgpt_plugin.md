## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Find animal transporters with the highest reviews and ratings near [address].

2. Check if the animal transporter named [company name] has any complaints registered against them.

3. Provide a list of animal transporters with a minimum [number] star rating near [address].

4. What are the average prices for animal transporters specializing in [type of animal] near [address]?

5. Can you recommend a reputable animal transporter for long-distance travel near [address]?

6. Search for animal transporters near [address] that offer climate-controlled services.

7. Identify animal transporters near [address] that have experience with transporting exotic animals.

8. Find the nearest emergency animal transporter services near [address].

9. Check if the animal transporter named [company name] offers insurance for transported animals.

10. Search for animal transporters that have been in operation for over [number] years near [address].
