## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Determine the price-to-earnings (P/E) ratio for the stock ticker symbol {ticker} over the period of {number_of_days}.

2. Calculate the dividend yield for the stock ticker symbol {ticker} over the past {number_of_days}.

3. Analyze the correlation between the stock ticker symbols {ticker1} and {ticker2} over the specified period {start_date} to {end_date}.

4. Identify trends in the stock price of the ticker symbol {ticker} using support and resistance levels over the period of {window_of_days}.

5. Evaluate the performance of the stock ticker symbol {ticker} compared to the benchmark index {benchmark_index} from {start_date} to {end_date}.

6. Retrieve the earnings per share (EPS) data for the stock ticker symbol {ticker} from the past {number_of_quarters} quarters.

7. Analyze the stock's return on investment (ROI) for the ticker symbol {ticker} over the period of {number_of_days}.

8. Compute the risk-adjusted return of the stock ticker symbol {ticker} using the Sharpe Ratio over the past {number_of_days}.

9. Identify historical stock splits for the ticker symbol {ticker} within the specified time frame {start_date} to {end_date}.

10. Create a price chart for the stock ticker symbol {ticker} including moving averages, RSI, and Bollinger Bands over the period of {number_of_days}.
