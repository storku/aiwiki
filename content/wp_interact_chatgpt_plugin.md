## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Fetch Blog Posts by Category: "Show me the latest 10 blog posts in the category {category} from the domain {domain}.

2. Fetch Blog Posts by Author: "Show me the latest 10 blog posts written by the author {author} on the domain {domain}.

3. Fetch Blog Posts by Tag: "Show me the latest 10 blog posts with the tag {tag} from the domain {domain}.

4. Fetch Blog Posts Between Specified Dates: "Show me blog posts published between {start_date} and {end_date} from the domain {domain}.

5. Fetch Blog Posts Sorted by Popularity: "Show me the top 10 most popular blog posts from the domain {domain}.

6. Fetch Blog Posts Sorted by Comments: "Show me the latest 10 blog posts on the domain {domain}, sorted by the number of comments.

7. Fetch Blog Posts with Specific Keyword and Category: "Show me the latest blog posts containing the term {search_term} and in the category {category} from the domain {domain}.

8. Fetch the Titles and Featured Images of Blog Posts: "Show me the titles and featured images of the latest 10 blog posts from the domain {domain}.

9. Fetch the Next Set of Blog Posts: "Show me the next 10 blog posts from the domain {domain} after the last post I viewed.

10. Display Blog Posts in Reader Mode: "Display the content of the latest 10 blog posts in reader mode from the domain {domain}.
