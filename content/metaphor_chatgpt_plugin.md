## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Can you suggest a popular book on time management and productivity?

2. Find me an online course that teaches beginner-level programming in Python.

3. I'm looking for an in-depth review of the latest iPhone model. Can you help me find one?

4. Provide a link to an engaging documentary on the history of ancient civilizations.

5. I need a list of must-visit tourist attractions in Europe. Can you find a reliable source?

6. Find me an article that discusses the benefits of mindfulness and meditation.

7. I'm interested in learning about the future of renewable energy. Can you find an expert's opinion piece?

8. Can you recommend a YouTube channel that offers exercise routines for seniors?

9. I'm looking for a beginner's guide to understanding cryptocurrency and its potential impact on the economy.

10. Find me a TED Talk that explores the importance of creativity in education.
