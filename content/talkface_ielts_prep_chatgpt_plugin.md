## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. I would like to practice an IELTS Speaking Part 1 topic that focuses on my daily routine.

2. Can you provide me with an IELTS Speaking Part 2 task that involves discussing a piece of technology?

3. I'm looking for an IELTS Speaking Part 1 topic that relates to food and cooking.

4. Please give me an IELTS Speaking Part 2 task that asks me to describe a memorable journey.

5. I'd like to practice an IELTS Speaking Part 1 topic that requires me to discuss my educational background.

6. Provide me with an IELTS Speaking Part 2 task that involves sharing a personal achievement.

7. I need an IELTS Speaking Part 1 topic that asks me about my preferences in music or movies.

8. Can you generate an IELTS Speaking Part 2 task that focuses on discussing a problem and its solution?

9. I'm interested in an IELTS Speaking Part 1 topic that involves talking about my hometown.

10. Please provide an IELTS Speaking Part 2 task that requires me to describe a favorite book or film.
