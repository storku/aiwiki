## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Search for entry-level jobs in {Industry} within a 25-mile radius of {City, State}.

2. Find remote freelance opportunities in the field of {Specific Field}.

3. Show me full-time jobs in {City, State} that offer visa sponsorship.

4. Look for part-time jobs in {City, State} with flexible hours.

5. Search for remote jobs offering professional development opportunities.

6. Find me entry-level software development positions in {City, State} requiring a Bachelor's degree.

7. Show me marketing positions in {City, State} that allow dogs in the office.

8. Look for jobs within a 20-mile radius of {City, State} with a minimum salary of ${Amount per Year}.

9. Search for full-time jobs in the field of {Industry} offering remote work options.

10. Find me internship opportunities for {Specific Major Studies} students in {City, State}.
