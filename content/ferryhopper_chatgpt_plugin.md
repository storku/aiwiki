## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Can you provide information on the ferry companies operating in {country}?

2. What are the popular ferry routes between {origin} and {destination}?

3. How can I book a ferry ticket from {origin} to {destination} on {dateStr}?

4. Are there any discounts or offers available for ferry tickets to {destination} in {country}?

5. Can you suggest the most scenic ferry routes in {country}?

6. How far in advance should I book my ferry ticket from {origin} to {destination} on {dateStr}?

7. Are there any overnight ferry options from {origin} to {destination} on {dateStr}?

8. Can you recommend the most affordable ferry service from {origin} to {destination} on {dateStr}?

9. Is it possible to travel with a vehicle on the ferry between {origin} and {destination}? If so, what are the costs?

10. Can you provide information about the onboard facilities of ferries operating between {origin} and {destination}?
