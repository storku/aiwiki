## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. View the remaining balance on my CLINQ account.

2. Update my profile information on my CLINQ account.

3. Enable call forwarding on my CLINQ account to [phone_number].

4. Disable call forwarding on my CLINQ account.

5. Fetch the list of received calls on CLINQ between [start_date] and [end_date].

6. Create a recurring call reminder on CLINQ for [contact_name] at [phone_number] every [day/week/month].

7. Check the call quality reports from my CLINQ account for the past month.

8. Retrieve the list of voicemails from my CLINQ account.

9. Mute all notifications from my CLINQ account for [duration].

10. Unmute notifications from my CLINQ account.
