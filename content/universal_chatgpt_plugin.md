## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Analyze the following PDF file and provide a summary: www.example.com/files/report.pdf.

2. What are the top 5 most visited websites globally as of today?

3. Find the highest-grossing movie worldwide in the month of September 2023.

4. Who are the top 10 influencers on Instagram with the most followers?

5. Visit the webpage at www.techblog.com/latest-gadgets and provide an overview of the most interesting new technology.

6. What are the top 3 trending topics on Reddit in the past 24 hours?

7. Generate a QR code for this WiFi network: SSID: MyNetwork, Password: MyPassword123.

8. What are the top 5 most popular songs on Spotify's global top 50 chart right now?

9. Visit the webpage at www.financewebsite.com/market-updates and provide a summary of the current stock market trends.

10. What is the weather forecast for London, United Kingdom, for the next 3 days?
