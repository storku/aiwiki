## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Can you analyze this text and provide me with the word count and character count (both with and without spaces)? Here is the text: {text}

2. I'd like to know the length of this text. Can you give me the number of words and characters (with and without spaces) for this: {text}?

3. I need a quick analysis of this text's length in terms of words and characters (including and excluding spaces). Please help! Here's the text: {text}

4. Could you determine the number of words and characters (with spaces and without spaces) in the following text: {text}?

5. I'm unsure about the number of words and character count (including and excluding spaces) of this text: {text}. Can you check for me?

6. Can you provide me with the word count and character count (both with and without spaces) for the following text: {text}?

7. I want to verify the length of this text: {text}. How many words and characters (with and without spaces) are there?

8. I am curious about the word and character count (with and without spaces) for this particular text: {text}. Can you share those numbers with me?

9. I need some help with counting words and characters (including and excluding spaces) in this text: {text}. Can you provide those numbers?

10. Can you assist me by calculating the number of words and characters (with spaces and without spaces) in this text: {text}?
