## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Create a Facebook Ad for {brand_name} featuring {product_name} with a captivating headline and persuasive call to action.

2. Generate an Instagram ad for the brand {brand_name}, showcasing {product_name} with a visually appealing design and strong messaging.

3. Help me write a LinkedIn Ad promoting {brand_name}'s {product_name} targeting professionals in {industry_name} with an emphasis on {unique_selling_proposition}.

4. Create a Twitter Ad for {brand_name} that promotes {product_name} with a catchy hashtag and a compelling call to action.

5. Analyze the website {website_url} and generate a carousel ad for Facebook with the key features of their {product_name}.

6. Draft an email campaign promoting {brand_name}'s new {product_name}. Include captivating subject line, engaging content and a strong call to action.

7. Develop a YouTube video ad script for {brand_name} that highlights the benefits of {product_name} and encourages viewers to try it.

8. Write a catchy headline and persuasive copy for a banner ad promoting {brand_name}'s {product_name} targeted at {audience_description}.

9. Generate a Pinterest ad for {brand_name} that showcases the visually appealing aspects of {product_name} and entices users to click.

10. Design an ad campaign for {brand_name} highlighting {product_name} for both mobile and desktop that has engaging visuals, strong messaging, and a powerful call to action.
