**NASA Media Explorer** is a [Plugin](/wiki/plugin) for [ChatGPT](/wiki/chatgpt).              

## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Search for images and videos related to the Juno mission to Jupiter.

2. Can you find media assets showcasing Earth as seen from space?

3. I'm interested in the Moon landings. Can you find images and videos from those historic moments?

4. Find me some captivating images of various nebulae.

5. Can you search for videos related to SpaceX's collaboration with NASA?

6. I'm curious about the Kepler space telescope. Can you find media assets related to its discoveries?

7. Search for images and videos related to comet missions like Rosetta and Deep Impact.

8. Can you find media assets related to famous astronaut crews, like the Mercury Seven or the Apollo 11 crew?

9. Find me some images of the recent solar eclipse or other astronomical events.

10. I'm interested in the Artemis program. Can you find images and videos related to future lunar missions?
