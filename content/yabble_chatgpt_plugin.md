## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Create a New Survey with Custom Demographics: "Create a new survey on the topic of {Survey Topic}, targeting demographics {Gender}, {Age}, {Location}, {Marital Status}, {Employment}, {Salary}, and {Children}, and provide me with the generated questions.

2. Export Survey Data: "Export the data from the survey with ID {Survey ID} to a CSV file and provide a download link.

3. View Survey Analytics: "Show me the analytics dashboard for the survey with ID {Survey ID}, including response rate, completion rate, and average time spent.

4. Pause an Ongoing Survey: "Pause the survey with ID {Survey ID} until further notice.

5. Resume a Paused Survey: "Resume the survey with ID {Survey ID} and start collecting responses again.

6. Send Survey Reminders: "Send a reminder email to those who haven't completed the survey with ID {Survey ID} yet.

7. Create a Survey Report: "Create a comprehensive report for the survey with ID {Survey ID}, including executive summary, data analysis, graphs, and recommendations.

8. Share Survey Results: "Share the results of the survey with ID {Survey ID} with {Email Address} or {Collaborator's Name}.

9. Clone a Survey: "Clone the survey with ID {Survey ID} and provide the new survey ID.

10. Cross Tab Analysis: "Perform cross tab analysis between question {Question ID 1} and question {Question ID 2} in the survey with ID {Survey ID} and provide key findings.
