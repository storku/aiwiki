### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:
Find me a romantic Italian restaurant for a date night this Friday.
Can you suggest a family-friendly seafood restaurant with outdoor seating this Saturday?
Show me the top 5 rated sushi restaurants available for lunch tomorrow.
I want to book a table for 6 at a Mexican restaurant with live music this Sunday night.
Recommend a vegetarian restaurant with gluten-free options for a birthday celebration next week.
Find an all-you-can-eat buffet restaurant available for lunch at 1 PM this Thursday.
Search for a French fine dining restaurant for a business dinner next Tuesday.
Show me brunch spots with bottomless mimosas available for this Sunday at noon.
Find a kid-friendly pizzeria with a private room for a birthday party in two weeks.
Look for a steakhouse with a great wine selection for a date next Wednesday night.
