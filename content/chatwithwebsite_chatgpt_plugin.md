## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Chat with Wikipedia: Tell me about the history of smartphones.

2. Chat with IMDb: List some popular movies from the early 2000s.

3. Chat with National Geographic: Explain the process of photosynthesis.

4. Chat with TripAdvisor: Suggest the top 5 tourist attractions in Paris.

5. Chat with Food Network: How do I cook a perfect steak?

6. Chat with WebMD: What are some common symptoms of the flu?

7. Chat with Scientific American: Describe Einstein's theory of relativity.

8. Chat with CNN: What are the top headlines today?

9. Chat with Forbes: How do I create a successful startup?

10. Chat with NASA: Tell me about the recent Mars rover mission.
