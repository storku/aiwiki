## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. How is the weather in {location} currently affecting visibility?

2. What are the expected high and low temperatures in {location} for the next three days?

3. Is there any chance of rain or snow in {location} over the next week?

4. What is the current humidity level in {location}?

5. Can you provide the current air quality index for {location}?

6. What is the sunrise and sunset time in {location} today?

7. I'm planning a picnic in {location} tomorrow. What will the weather be like?

8. What's the dew point in {location} right now?

9. How much precipitation is expected in {location} within the next 24 hours?

10. Is {location} expected to have any severe weather conditions in the coming days?
