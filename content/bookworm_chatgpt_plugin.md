## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Can you suggest a book with a ${profession} protagonist in a ${genre} setting?

2. Recommend a book for fans of ${book_series}.

3. What are some popular ${genre} books released in the past ${time_period}?

4. I'm looking for a book with a ${character_trait} antagonist. Can you help?

5. What are some lesser-known but highly praised ${genre} novels?

6. I would like to read a book with a strong friendship theme in a ${genre} setting. Any suggestions?

7. Which books should I read if I love ${author_name}'s writing style?

8. Can you recommend a gripping $genre series with at least ${number} books?

9. What are some ${genre} novels that have been adapted into successful movies or TV shows?

10. I would like a book that focuses on ${topic} from the perspective of ${perspective}. Can you suggest one?
