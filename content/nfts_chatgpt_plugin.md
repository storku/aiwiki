## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. What is the most popular NFT in the {collection name} collection?

2. Which NFT in the {collection name} collection has the largest number of owners in its trade history?

3. What is the most recent sale price of the NFT named {NFT name} in the {collection name} collection?

4. What is the rarity ranking of the NFT named {NFT name} in the {collection name} collection?

5. What are the most common attributes of NFTs in the {collection name} collection?

6. Which artists have contributed to the {collection name} collection?

7. How does the {collection name} collection compare to other similar NFT collections in terms of market value or popularity?

8. What is the total volume of sales in the {collection name} NFT collection?

9. How many unique buyers have participated in the {collection name} NFT collection's trade history?

10. What are some notable or high-profile sales or acquisitions within the {collection name} NFT collection?
