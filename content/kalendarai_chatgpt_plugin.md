## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Create a sales pitch using KalendarAI for my {product_name}.

2. Analyze the performance of my sales agents using KalendarAI insights.

3. Display a list of all the potential customers contacted through KalendarAI.

4. Export the customer feedback from sales interactions, ordered by date, using KalendarAI.

5. Obtain the revenue generated by sales agents in my KalendarAI account in the past month.

6. Prepare a sales presentation with KalendarAI to showcase {product_name} benefits.

7. Connect my CRM platform to KalendarAI for seamless sales agent integration.

8. Generate a report on KalendarAI's impact on my company's revenue growth.

9. Demonstrate how KalendarAI can increase conversion rates for my business.

10. Provide a cost analysis of using KalendarAI in comparison to a traditional sales team.
