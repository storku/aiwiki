## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Retrieve the total supply of the [symbol] token on [chain_name].

2. Find the circulating supply of the [symbol] token on [chain_id].

3. Look up the ICO price of the [symbol] token on [chain_name].

4. Fetch the explorer link for the contract at [contract_address] on [chain_id].

5. Find the owner address of the [symbol] token on [chain_name].

6. Get the latest list of ERC20 tokens indexed on [chain_name].

7. Retrieve the volume of the [symbol] token in the last 24 hours on [chain_id].

8. List all ERC20 tokens with a market cap above [market_cap_value].

9. Search for ERC20 tokens listed on [dex_name] on [chain_id].

10. Compare the market price of [symbol] on both [chain_name_1] and [chain_name_2].
