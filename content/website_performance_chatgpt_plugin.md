## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Evaluate the overall user experience on my website at {website_url}.

2. Identify areas of improvement for my website's SEO at {website_url}.

3. Determine if my website at {website_url} meets WCAG accessibility guidelines.

4. What optimizations can I make to my website at {website_url} to enhance user engagement?

5. Analyze the performance and load times of my website at {website_url} across different browsers.

6. Are there any broken links or other issues affecting the SEO performance of my website at {website_url}?

7. Detect potential security vulnerabilities on my website at {website_url}.

8. Provide a mobile-first design audit for my website at {website_url}.

9. How does the website {website_url} stack up against its competitors in terms of performance and best practices?

10. What is the time-to-first-byte (TTFB) for my website at {website_url}?
