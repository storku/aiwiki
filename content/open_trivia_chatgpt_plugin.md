## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Fetch me 7 trivia questions from the category of 'Sports'.

2. Can you provide me with 10 trivia questions of the 'multiple' type from the category of 'Entertainment: Television'?

3. Fetch me 15 trivia questions from the category of 'Science: Gadgets' with a medium difficulty level.

4. Can you provide me with 8 trivia questions of the 'boolean' type from the category of 'Mythology'?

5. Fetch me 12 trivia questions from the category of 'Art' with a hard difficulty level.

6. Can you provide me with 5 trivia questions of the 'multiple' type from the category of 'Science: Space'?

7. Fetch me 10 trivia questions from the category of 'Animals' with an easy difficulty level.

8. Can you provide me with 20 trivia questions of the 'boolean' type from the category of 'Politics'?

9. Fetch me 6 trivia questions from the category of 'Entertainment: Books' with a medium difficulty level.

10. Can you provide me with 10 trivia questions of the 'multiple' type from the category of 'Celebrities'?
