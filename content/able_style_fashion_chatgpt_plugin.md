## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Suggest a womenswear outfit suitable for a rainy day.

2. What should I wear for a casual business meeting in menswear today? It's cool outside.

3. Give me two outfit suggestions for womenswear suitable for a beach party.

4. What are some versatile menswear outfits for unpredictable weather?

5. Suggest a womenswear outfit suitable for a formal event in cold weather.

6. What should I wear to a summer wedding as a guest? I prefer womenswear.

7. Give me three outfit suggestions for athletic menswear appropriate for outdoor activities.

8. What are some chic womenswear outfits for a night out in warm weather?

9. Suggest a unisex outfit suitable for a cozy evening indoors.

10. What should I wear today? I prefer gender-neutral clothing and the temperature is mild.
