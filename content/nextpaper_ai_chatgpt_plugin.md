## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. What is the most recent research paper published on ${topic}?

2. Please fetch the latest paper in the field of ${topic}.

3. Find me the newest research paper related to ${topic}.

4. Identify the latest scientific publication on ${topic}.

5. Obtain the most current research paper on ${topic} for me.

6. Can you locate the newest published paper on ${topic}?

7. I'd like to read the latest study on ${topic}. Can you find it?

8. Track down the most recent paper on ${topic}.

9. I'd appreciate the latest scientific article on ${topic}.

10. Discover the newest paper published about ${topic} for me.
