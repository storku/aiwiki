**Bibliography Crossref** is a [Plugin](/wiki/plugin) for [ChatGPT](/wiki/chatgpt).              

## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Retrieve all publications from the current year related to ${topic}.

2. Find publications from the past 5 years with at least ${number} citations for ${subject}.

3. Locate review articles related to ${topic}.

4. Get a list of books and chapters written by ${author_name}.

5. Provide the publication date of the article with DOI: ${doi}.

6. Search for conference proceedings on the topic ${subject}.

7. Find the first article ever published on ${topic}.

8. Retrieve the metadata of the publication with ISSN: ${issn}.

9. Compile a list of the most influential publications for ${keyword} from the past decade.

10. Find articles from the journal ${journal_name} on ${subject}.
