## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. What are the common symptoms of dengue fever and how can it be prevented in the Philippines?

2. Can you provide information on the COVID-19 vaccination rollout plan in the Philippines?

3. What steps should be taken in case of a natural disaster (e.g. typhoon, earthquake) to ensure proper health and safety measures in the Philippines?

4. How can one access mental health resources and support services in the Philippines?

5. What are the health risks associated with air pollution in the Philippines, and how can they be minimized?

6. What are the common causes of malnutrition among children in the Philippines, and what measures are being taken to address this issue?

7. What are the guidelines for hygiene practices to prevent the spread of illnesses in the public transportation system of the Philippines?

8. How can individuals protect themselves from mosquito-borne diseases in the Philippines?

9. What general health tips do you have for tourists traveling to the Philippines?

10. What measures has the Philippine Department of Health implemented to address the maternal mortality rate in the country?
