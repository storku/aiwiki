## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. What are some tasty slow cooker recipes I can try?

2. I have ground beef, pasta, and tomato sauce. What recipe can I make using these ingredients?

3. Can you recommend some low-carb dinner ideas?

4. I'm hosting a dinner party, and need a fancy appetizer idea. What would you suggest?

5. What are some delicious recipes with seasonal vegetables?

6. Show me some recipes that incorporate zucchini noodles.

7. I want to try some new seafood recipes. Can you find some options for me?

8. Can you find me a simple recipe for a homemade chocolate cake?

9. How to make authentic {Regional_Cuisine} dishes at home?

10. What are some quick and healthy recipes I can make for a working lunch?
