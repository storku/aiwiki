## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Please help me analyze the eligibility requirements for the clinical trial [trialID]. Can you provide any medical terms and explanations?

2. Can you parse the inclusion and exclusion criteria for the clinical trial [trialID] and provide me with a summary?

3. I need to know the eligibility conditions for the clinical trial [trialID]. Can you give me a thorough explanation?

4. Please provide me with a clear overview of the medical aspects of the eligibility criteria for the clinical trial [trialID].

5. Could you help me learn more about the eligibility criteria, such as age, gender, and medical conditions, in the clinical trial [trialID]?

6. I'd like to better understand the medical specifics of the eligibility criteria for the clinical trial [trialID]. Can you assist?

7. I am interested in examining the medical components of the eligibility criteria for the clinical trial [trialID]. Can you provide further insights?

8. Can you elaborate on the medical conditions and treatments mentioned in the eligibility criteria for the clinical trial [trialID]?

9. Could you help me gather key eligibility criteria information for the clinical trial [trialID], including medical terms and their meaning?

10. Please analyze the medical details in the eligibility criteria for the clinical trial [trialID] and provide an easy-to-understand explanation.
