## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Examine the sales copy at {website_url} and offer suggestions for improvement.

2. Help me optimize the sales copy on this webpage: {page_url}.

3. Check the sales copy on this website: {website_url} and provide enhancement ideas.

4. What revisions can improve the sales copy on this page: {page_url}?

5. Can you give me tips to better the sales copy at {website_url}?

6. Review and suggest upgrades for the sales copy on this URL: {page_url}.

7. Please assess and recommend improvements for the sales copy at {website_url}.

8. I need assistance with optimizing sales copy on this page: {page_url}.

9. Suggest ideas to spruce up the sales copy on this website: {website_url}.

10. Identify areas of improvement for the sales copy on this page: {page_url}.
