## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:  

**Analyze**

1. Analyze Alphabet Inc. (GOOGL)

2. SONY’s latest stock Price Trend

3. Find the latest P/E ratio for HSBC

4. Samsung Electronics' latest quarterly Performance

5. Business Description of Hindustan Unilever, use boolio

**Opinion**

1. What's the Boolio Score of NVDA?

2. Tell me the Guru Score for META stock

3. Sentiment Analysis based on news and social media sources for NFLX

4. Provide Valuation Reports for AAPL under various scenarios

5. Hyundai Motor Company SWOT Analysis , use Boolio

6. How Risky is TSLA?

7. Compare Google and Microsoft, with table format

8. Who are FORD's Competitors?

9. Analyst's Opinion on Amazon

10. Correlation with Economic Indicators and  Coca-Cola Company performance

11. Research and analyze stock NESTLE for me. Provide financial Performance, Risk assessment, and potential investment Opportunities

**Recommend**

1. Top 10 UK stocks that fit Peter Lynch's style

2. India stocks with a PER of 10 or less and a Market Capitalization of at least $100 million

3. Please recommend 10 stocks in the Korean Healthcare Sector
