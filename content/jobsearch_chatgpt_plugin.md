## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Search for internship opportunities in the engineering field in Berlin, Germany.

2. Find me a list of full-time customer service jobs in Munich, Germany that were posted in the last month.

3. Show me recent job openings at Siemens in Frankfurt, Germany.

4. I'm looking for marketing manager positions in Hamburg, Germany.

5. Can you find remote graphic design positions that were published in the last two weeks in Germany?

6. Search for environmental science jobs in Düsseldorf, Germany with a start date within the next 90 days.

7. I'm interested in human resources roles in Cologne, Germany.

8. Find me job openings at Bosch that were published in the last 10 days in Stuttgart, Germany.

9. I'm looking for pharmaceutical jobs in Nuremberg, Germany that start within the next 45 days.

10. Can you find job openings in the automotive industry in Leipzig, Germany?
