## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Show me all my reminders for the next {Number} days.

2. Delete all reminders tagged with '{Tag}'.

3. Update the date and time for the reminder '{Task}' to {New Date and Time}.

4. Add a reminder to '{Task}' every {Day/Week/Month} starting on {Date and Time}.

5. Show me all my reminders due today.

6. Find the reminder related to '{Keyword}'.

7. Reorder my reminders by priority or date.

8. List all reminders tagged with '{Tag1}' and '{Tag2}'.

9. Snooze the reminder for '{Task}' by {Number} minutes/hours.

10. Add a note to the reminder '{Task}' with '{Note}'.
