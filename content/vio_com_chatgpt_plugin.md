Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

4. Search for hotels in {destination} within walking distance of public transportation.

5. Find family-friendly resorts in {destination} with kids' club and pool.

6. Show me bed and breakfasts in {destination} with ensuite bathrooms and free parking.

7. Find accommodations in {destination} that allow long-term stays.

8. Search for hotels in {destination} with flexible cancellation policies.

9. Find eco-friendly lodging options in {destination}.

10. Show me all-inclusive resorts in {destination} with beach access and water sports facilities.
