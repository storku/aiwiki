## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Find a Machine Learning Engineer: "Search for a Machine Learning Engineer with at least 3 years of experience, proficient in TensorFlow and Python. The position is based in Europe and the budget is $110,000.

2. Locate a System Administrator: "I need a System Administrator with expertise in Linux and Windows Server management. They should have a minimum of 4 years of experience. The position is open to candidates from Oceania.

3. Search for an Embedded Systems Engineer: "Find an Embedded Systems Engineer proficient in C/C++ and microcontroller programming with a minimum of 5 years of experience. The position is open worldwide and the budget is $90,000.

4. Hire a Database Administrator: "I'm looking for a Database Administrator with expertise in MySQL and PostgreSQL, and at least 3 years of experience. The position is based in North America.

5. Find a Blockchain Developer: "Search for a Blockchain Developer proficient in Solidity, Ethereum, and smart contracts with a minimum of 4 years of experience. The position is open to candidates from Asia.

6. Locate a Network Engineer: "I need a Network Engineer with expertise in Cisco and Juniper networking equipment. They should have a minimum of 5 years of experience. The position is open to candidates from Europe.

7. Search for a Web Designer: "Find a Web Designer proficient in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript with a minimum of 2 years of experience. The position is open worldwide.

8. Hire a Cloud Architect: "I'm looking for a Cloud Architect with expertise in GCP and Azure and at least 4 years of experience. The position is based in North America.

9. Find a Software Tester: "Search for a Software Tester proficient in manual and automated testing with a minimum of 3 years of experience. The position is open to candidates from Oceania.

10. Locate a Firmware Engineer: "I need a Firmware Engineer with expertise in embedded C programming and ARM Cortex microcontrollers. They should have a minimum of 5 years of experience. The position is open to candidates from Europe.
