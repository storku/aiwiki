## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. What is the recent stock performance of ${company_name}?

2. Provide a list of competitors for ${company_name} in the ${sector} sector.

3. Give me a summary of the recent financial performance of ${company_name}.

4. How has the ${sector} sector performed in the stock market over the past ${number_of_days} days?

5. What are the upcoming events or catalysts for ${company_name}?

6. Identify potential risks and opportunities for ${company_name} in the ${sector} sector.

7. Analyze the management team of ${company_name} and their past performance.

8. Give me a comparison of ${company_name}'s performance with its competitors in the ${sector} sector.

9. What are the future growth prospects and revenue projections for ${company_name}?

10. Find me recent analyst recommendations and price target updates for ${company_name}.
