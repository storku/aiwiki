## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Tell me if there are any scheduled roadworks for {road} this week.

2. What is the status of speed cameras on {road} this evening?

3. Display all speed camera and roadwork locations in South Australia for the next 24 hours.

4. Identify any variable speed camera zones on {road} today.

5. Inform me of any road closures on {road} due to roadworks this weekend.

6. Are there any speed cameras active on my way to {destination} through {road1} and {road2}?

7. Provide updates on roadworks affecting traffic on {road} today.

8. What are the chances of encountering a roadwork zone along {road} this afternoon?

9. List all the South Australian roads with planned roadworks for the upcoming week.

10. Display mobile speed camera locations in my area today.
