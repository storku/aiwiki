## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Discover artwork in the Met's collection featuring {subject_matter}.

2. Find me notable artwork from the Romantic era in the Met's collection.

3. Search for any Japanese ukiyo-e prints in the Met's collection.

4. Locate any available artwork from the Abstract Expressionist movement in the Met's collection.

5. Identify any available illuminated manuscripts in the Met's collection.

6. Find any prehistoric art pieces in the Met's collection.

7. Show me a variety of American Folk Art pieces from the Met's collection.

8. Retrieve any medieval weaponry found in the Met's collection.

9. Explore landscape paintings from the Met's collection created during the {time_period}.

10. Uncover any available portrait paintings from the Met's collection created in the {century}.
