## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. What are the current market predictions from top traders for [Company Name]?

2. Can you provide the latest technical analysis ideas for stocks in the renewable energy sector?

3. What are the top traders' opinions on the recent market movements of [Specific Stock Ticker]?

4. Show me the latest technical analysis ideas for index funds from top traders.

5. What are top traders' suggestions for short-term trading strategies in the cryptocurrency market?

6. Provide me with the latest trends and insights for the stock market this week.

7. What are the latest technical analysis ideas for small-cap stocks from top traders?

8. Give me the top traders' insights on recent developments in the [Industry Name] industry and their impact on stocks.

9. What are the latest technical analysis ideas for altcoins from top traders?

10. Show me the latest risk management strategies from top traders for both stocks and cryptocurrencies.
