## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. What are the top tourist attractions in ${city}?

2. Show me the popular new movies released this month.

3. Find the current best-selling books in ${genre}.

4. What are upcoming concerts in ${city} this month?

5. What is the scientific explanation of ${phenomenon}?

6. What are the nutritional benefits of ${food}?

7. Find out the latest breakthroughs in ${research_field} research.

8. What are the top reviewed restaurants in ${city}?

9. Locate the nearest ${type_of_place} around my current location.

10. What are the top-grossing video games in ${platform}?
