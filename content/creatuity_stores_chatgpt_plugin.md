## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Find me the best-rated yoga mats in the price range of $20 to $100.

2. Search for eco-friendly clothing brands for men and women under $150.

3. I'm looking for the top-rated noise-canceling headphones within a budget of $50 to $350.

4. Show me a list of popular home office furniture options, including desks and chairs.

5. Can you find me the best deals on tablets from brands like Apple and Samsung?

6. Search for highly rated pet supplies for dogs and cats, including toys and accessories.

7. I'm looking for high-quality activewear from brands like Nike or Adidas.

8. Find me the top 10 award-winning movies and TV series available for streaming.

9. I'm looking for smart home devices from brands like Amazon and Google under $150.

10. Search for the latest gardening tools and accessories for under $100.
