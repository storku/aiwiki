## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Find entry-level {job title} jobs in {city}, {state} for recent graduates with a {specific degree}.

2. I'm looking for {full-time/part-time} {job title} jobs at {company name} in {city}, {state}.

3. Can you please suggest {industry} jobs in {city}, {state} that require travel or a high level of interaction with clients?

4. I want to find {job title} positions in {city}, {state} that emphasize {specific skill or quality, for example strong communication skills, teamwork}.

5. Search for {job title} jobs within {distance} miles of {city}, {state}, and list the ones that provide training and development programs.

6. I'm looking for jobs in the fields of {industry1} and {industry2} in {city}, {state} that pay at least {desired salary} per year.

7. Can you find me entry-level jobs in {city}, {state} that offer a clear career progression within the {industry}?

8. I'm interested in finding {job title} positions in {city}, {state} that have a strong focus on {specific area, for example sustainability, innovation}.

9. Search for {job title} jobs at {specific company or companies} in {city}, {state} offering a signing bonus or relocation assistance.

10. Can you help me find remote {job title} jobs in {city}, {state} that require no more than {years} years of experience and allow a flexible work schedule?
