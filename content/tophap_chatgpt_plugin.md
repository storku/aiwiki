## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Show me the property listings in {school_district} with at least {school_rating} school rating.

2. I'm looking for commercial properties in {city} that were listed in the last {time_period}. Can you help?

3. Find me properties within a {walking_distance} walking distance to {amenity_name} in {city}.

4. What are the crime rates for the neighborhood around {property_address}?

5. Show me a list of properties in {city} that have a lot size between {lot_size_min} and {lot_size_max}.

6. Find me properties close to public transportation in {city} with at least {bed_min} bedrooms.

7. I'm looking for properties with a pool for purchase in {city}. Can you help?

8. Show me properties within a {commute_time} commute time to {workplace_address}.

9. I need to see properties in {city} that have been recently renovated. Can you find them for me?

10. Find me pet-friendly condos for rent in {city} within a budget of {rent_budget}.
