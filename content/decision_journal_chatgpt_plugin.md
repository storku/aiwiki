## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Update the decision with ID {decision_id} to add a new alternative {alternative}.

2. Show me the top 5 decisions with the highest accuracy scores.

3. List all my decisions that have been reviewed within the last {time_period}.

4. Update the next review date for the decision with ID {decision_id} to {new_next_review_date}.

5. Create a new decision and ask me for the title, context, and expected outcome.

6. Show me all my decisions that were made in the {time_period}.

7. Find decisions where the actual outcome differed significantly from the expected outcome.

8. Compare the decision with ID {decision_id_1} and the decision with ID {decision_id_2} in terms of their accuracy scores and outcomes.

9. List all my decisions that involve the keyword {keyword} in both the context and expected outcome.

10. Generate a report on my overall decision-making progress, including average accuracy score and most common outcomes.
