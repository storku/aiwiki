## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Find a list of top 5 motivational TED Talks on YouTube.

2. Show me a tutorial on how to fold origami animals.

3. What are some popular music videos released in the 2021?

4. Recommend travel vlogs featuring European destinations.

5. Find a compilation of the most memorable moments from the Olympics.

6. Show me some of the best cooking channels on YouTube for beginners.

7. What are some funny video clips of animals interacting with each other?

8. Discover popular YouTube channels discussing mental health and self-care.

9. Find a video tutorial on creating a successful workout plan for beginners.

10. Show me some videos of inspiring commencement speeches by famous personalities.
