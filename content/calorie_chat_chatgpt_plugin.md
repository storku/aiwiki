## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Log my afternoon snack today. I had a small fruit salad with strawberries, grapes, and pineapple.

2. Can you calculate the total calories I consumed yesterday based on my food logs?

3. Add a food log for my breakfast today, including two scrambled eggs, two strips of bacon, and a slice of whole grain toast.

4. I want to update my lunch log. Instead of a turkey sandwich, I had a tuna salad wrap.

5. Show me a summary of my average daily calorie intake for the past month.

6. I need to correct the carbohydrate content for my dinner tonight. It should be 45 grams, not 30.

7. Add a food log for my mid-morning snack. I had a handful of almonds and a banana.

8. Can you display my food logs for June 1st to June 5th?

9. I want to update the fat count for my lunch today. It should be 15 grams, not 10.

10. Remove the dessert log for three days ago. I decided not to eat the ice cream.
