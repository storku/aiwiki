## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Calculate the present value of an annuity with a future value of $[future_value], an annual interest rate of [rate]%, and [periods] periods.

2. Find the median of the given numbers: [number1], [number2], [number3], [number4], [number5].

3. Calculate the area of a triangle with base [base] units and height [height] units.

4. Determine the slope of a line passing through the points ([x1], [y1]) and ([x2], [y2]).

5. Calculate the surface area of a sphere with a radius of [radius] units.

6. Find the nth term of a geometric sequence with a first term of [a1] and a common ratio of [r] after [n] terms.

7. Calculate the perimeter of a rectangle with a length of [length] units and a width of [width] units.

8. Find the square root of [number].

9. Determine the angle in degrees for a right triangle with adjacent side [adjacent] units and opposite side [opposite] units.

10. Calculate the volume of a cone with a radius of [radius] units and a height of [height] units.
