## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Design a creature that would inhabit the stormy skies of a world filled with floating islands.

2. I'm setting a sci-fi novel in a planet with dense jungles. Can you create a creature that would fit into this setting?

3. What kind of creature might I encounter if I were exploring the marshlands of a supernatural realm?

4. I'm creating a board game with a volcanic island theme. Can you generate a creature that players might have to face?

5. What kind of creature would make its home among the surreal rock formations of an alien canyon?

6. I'm developing a card game. Can you create a creature that players might summon to battle in a haunted graveyard?

7. What kind of creature might dwell in the enchanted groves of a magical realm?

8. Design a creature that can live in the harsh environment of a windswept tundra and still thrive.

9. I'm writing a comic book set in a futuristic cityscape. What kind of creature might lurk in the shadows of the towering buildings?

10. Create a creature that might inhabit the vibrant coral reefs of a fantastical ocean world.
