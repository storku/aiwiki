## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Find job opportunities for software engineers in the {industry_name} industry located in {location_name}.

2. Search for part-time and full-time jobs in the field of {field_name} in the Web3 industry.

3. Find talents with experience in {number_of_years} years in the {skill_name} field in the blockchain industry located in {location_name}.

4. Search for internship opportunities in the field of {field_name} in the crypto industry located in {location_name}.

5. Find talents with a minimum of {number_of_years} years of experience in the {skill_name} field in the Web3 industry.

6. Search for entry-level job opportunities in the field of {field_name} in the crypto industry.

7. Find talents with a strong background in {background_name} and experience in {experience_requirement} in the blockchain industry.

8. Search for job opportunities in the field of {field_name} in the Web3 industry that provide comprehensive training.

9. Find talents with excellent communication skills and a background in {background_name} in the crypto industry.

10. Search for job opportunities in the field of {field_name} in the blockchain industry that offer a flexible work schedule.
