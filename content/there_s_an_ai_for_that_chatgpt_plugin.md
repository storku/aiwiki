## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Identify AI tools that can automate the process of video editing.

2. What AI tools are available for translation and language learning applications?

3. Can you suggest AI tools that perform well in facial recognition tasks?

4. I'm interested in AI tools that are recognized for their capabilities in music generation.

5. Show me AI tools that excel in detecting health issues from medical images.

6. What AI tools are on the market that offer intelligent recommendation systems?

7. Can you recommend AI tools that are well-regarded for their document summarization features?

8. I'm looking for AI tools that have advanced capabilities in emotion recognition.

9. Find me AI tools that are notable for their social media monitoring and management abilities.

10. What are the best AI tools that can assist with improving cybersecurity?
