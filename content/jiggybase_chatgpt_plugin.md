## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Can you find any information about {keyword} in my collections?

2. Provide a summary of documents related to {keyword} from my collections.

3. Show me the top 3 collections containing information on {keyword}.

4. Help me find collections that focus on {keyword}.

5. Search my document collections for any relevant information on {keyword}.

6. List the collections in which {keyword} is a major topic.

7. What are the most recent documents mentioning {keyword} in my collections?

8. Can you help me find any supporting evidence on {keyword} from my collections?

9. Search my collections for case studies or examples related to {keyword}.

10. Identify key information about {keyword} from my document collections.
