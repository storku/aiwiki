## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Are there any popular coupon codes currently available for {store_name}?

2. I want to save money at {store_name}. Can you find me some valid coupon codes?

3. Can you provide me with the latest coupon codes for {store_name}?

4. I'm looking to get the best deals at {store_name}. Can you find some active coupon codes for me?

5. Could you help me find the most popular coupons for {store_name} at the moment?

6. I'm in need of some coupon codes for {store_name}. Can you help me find the best ones?

7. Can you search for the latest and most effective coupons for {store_name}?

8. I'm shopping online at {store_name} and need some coupon codes. Can you find me some?

9. Can you recommend some promo codes for {store_name} that have the highest savings?

10. I need the most recent coupon codes for {store_name}. Can you show me what's available?
