## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Create a time-travel game set in the ${historical_period} with ${historical_person} as my guide.

2. Begin a game in the ${historical_event} and provide me with a special ability to use: ${special_ability}.

3. Take me on an adventure in the ${historical_period}, and I would like to meet ${historical_person}.

4. Design a time-travel game where I can witness the ${historical_event} firsthand.

5. Set up an adventure for me in the ${historical_period} with the objective of meeting ${historical_person}.

6. Begin a game in the ${historical_event} and give me a mission to accomplish.

7. I want to play a game in the ${historical_period} where I have to solve a mystery involving ${historical_person}.

8. Set up a time-travel escapade in ${historical_period} with these challenges: ${challenge1}, ${challenge2}, ${challenge3}.

9. I'd like a game set in ${historical_event} with the goal of discovering a hidden piece of history.

10. Start a time-travel adventure in ${historical_period} where my objective is to protect ${historical_person}.
