**P-E For NFTs** is a [Plugin](/wiki/plugin) for [ChatGPT](/wiki/chatgpt).              

## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Calculate the price to earnings ratio for the NFT collection {token} over the last 12 months.

2. Display the top 10 highest earning NFTs in the collection {token}.

3. What is the overall sentiment about the NFT collection {token} in the market?

4. Compare the price to earnings ratio of the NFT collection {token} to {another_token}.

5. Graph the price to earnings ratio trend of the NFT collection {token} over the past year.

6. What are the major factors influencing the price to earnings ratio of the NFT collection {token}?

7. Provide a breakdown of the price per NFT in the collection {token}, including highs, lows, and averages.

8. List the rarest NFTs in the collection {token} and their respective price to earnings ratios.

9. What are the most active trading platforms for the NFT collection {token}?

10. Show the percentage change in the price to earnings ratio of the NFT collection {token} over the previous 6-month period.
