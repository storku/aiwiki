### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. What are the top Sci-fi movies trending on Netflix?

2. What are the best podcasts to listen to right now?

3. What are some binge-worthy TV shows I can watch this weekend?

4. What new releases should I check out in the world of books?

5. What are the top 10 highest-rated movies of all time?

6. Which critically acclaimed TV shows should I add to my watchlist?

7. What are some underrated movies I might have missed in the last few years?

8. What are the best true crime podcasts to listen to?

9. What are some must-read biographies or memoirs?

10. What are the top documentaries trending on streaming platforms?
