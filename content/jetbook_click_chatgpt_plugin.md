## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Search for the earliest flight from ${departure_city} to ${destination_city} on ${date}.

2. Find round-trip flights from ${departure_city} to ${destination_city} departing on ${departure_date} and returning on ${return_date}.

3. What are the visa requirements for ${nationality} citizens traveling to ${destination_country}?

4. Tell me about the must-visit tourist attractions in ${destination_city}.

5. Find a flight from ${departure_city} to ${destination_city} on ${date} that arrives before ${time}.

6. What are the current COVID-19 restrictions and requirements for traveling to ${destination_country}?

7. Compare flight prices for ${airline_1} and ${airline_2} from ${departure_city} to ${destination_city} on ${date}.

8. Tell me about the local customs and etiquettes in ${destination_country}.

9. Search for flights with flexible dates from ${departure_city} to ${destination_city} around ${preferred_date}.

10. What is the local currency of ${destination_country} and its exchange rate with ${base_currency}?
