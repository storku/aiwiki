### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Find the best deals on 2023 SUVs near Boston, Massachusetts.

2. List the safety features of the 2023 Honda Accord.

3. Compare the fuel efficiency of the 2023 Toyota Prius and 2023 Hyundai Ioniq.

4. How much is the average price for a 2023 Chevrolet Corvette in Miami, Florida?

5. What are the top 5 electric vehicles for sale in Seattle, Washington?

6. Provide me with the specifications and features of the 2023 Porsche Taycan.

7. Show me the best-rated luxury sedans for sale in Chicago, Illinois for the year 2023.

8. Find the most fuel-efficient cars available for sale in Austin, Texas for the year 2023.

9. Give me a list of all the 2023 pickup trucks ranked by towing capacity.

10. What are the different trim levels and their prices for the 2023 Subaru Outback?
