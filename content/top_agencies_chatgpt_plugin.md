## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Find me a design agency in {City}, {State}, {Country} focusing on {Service_List}

2. I'm looking for a top-rated ({Rating}) content marketing agency in {City}, {State}

3. Can you recommend a branding agency that offers {Service_List}?

4. Show me top marketing and design agencies in {City}, {State}, {Country} by ratings

5. Find me a {Rating}-rated SEO agency in {City}, {State}

6. I'm looking for a creative agency in {City}, {State}, {Country} that provides {Service_List}

7. Can you recommend a social media marketing agency in {City}, {State}?

8. Show me design agencies in {City} that specialize in {Service_List}

9. Find me an e-commerce agency in {City}, {State}, {Country} with a rating of at least {Rating}

10. I'm looking for a video production agency in {City}, {State} that offers {Service_List}
