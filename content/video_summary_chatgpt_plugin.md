## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Please provide a brief rundown of this YouTube video: {video_url}.

2. What are the most important parts of this YouTube video: {video_url}?

3. Give me a quick synopsis of this YouTube video: {video_url}.

4. Can you analyze this YouTube video: {video_url} and provide a summary of its content?

5. Can you create an outline based on this YouTube video: {video_url}?

6. Highlight the primary points made in this YouTube video: {video_url}.

7. Can you provide a concise breakdown of this YouTube video: {video_url}?

8. What are the main ideas discussed in this YouTube video: {video_url}?

9. Can you briefly explain the content of this YouTube video: {video_url}?

10. Please give me a short description of this YouTube video: {video_url}.
