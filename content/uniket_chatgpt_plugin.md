## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Search for products related to {query} on Uniket and filter by color {color}.

2. Search for products related to {query} on Uniket and filter by brand {brand}.

3. Find eco-friendly products related to {query} on Uniket.

4. Search for products related to {query} on Uniket with an average rating of {rating} or higher.

5. Apply promo code {promo_code} to my cart on Uniket.

6. Search for products related to {query} on Uniket that have free shipping.

7. Find customizable products related to {query} on Uniket.

8. Search for limited edition products related to {query} on Uniket.

9. Find gift options related to {query} on Uniket within the price range of {minimum_price} to {maximum_price}.

10. Search for products related to {query} on Uniket and filter them by those with a {warranty_duration}-year warranty or longer.
