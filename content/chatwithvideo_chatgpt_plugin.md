## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Determine the uploader's name for the YouTube video with the ID video_id.

2. Analyze the YouTube video with the ID video_id and evaluate its educational value.

3. What are the top three most-liked comments on the YouTube video with the ID video_id?

4. Identify any musical tracks or background music used in the YouTube video with the ID video_id.

5. Show me a list of timestamps/indexes for the key sections or chapters in the YouTube video with the ID video_id.

6. What are the primary visual elements, such as design or animation styles, in the YouTube video with the ID video_id?

7. Find related videos or channels for the YouTube video with the ID video_id.

8. What are some of the controversial or debated points mentioned in the comments of the YouTube video with the ID video_id?

9. What is the demographic of the target audience for the YouTube video with the ID video_id?

10. What is the average watch time or retention rate for the YouTube video with the ID video_id?
