## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Outdoor Dining: "Search for restaurants with outdoor seating in {city_name}.

2. Child-friendly Options: "Find child-friendly restaurants in {city_name} for dinner tonight.

3. Discover Art Galleries: "Show me a list of popular art galleries in {city_name}.

4. Weekend Brunch Locations: "Find the best places for weekend brunch in {city_name}.

5. Local Event Venues: "Search for event venues near {zip_code} suitable for a private party.

6. Live Music Spots: "Find bars and restaurants with live music in {city_name}.

7. Health and Wellness: "Search for yoga studios and wellness centers in {city_name}.

8. Best Coffee Shops: "Find the top-rated coffee shops in {city_name}.

9. Gift Shopping: "Show me gift shops with a local flair in {city_name}.

10. Farmers Markets: "Search for farmers markets and organic food stores in {city_name}.
