## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Find me reviews or meta-analyses related to {research_topic}.

2. Search for recent breakthroughs or discoveries in {research_topic}.

3. I need research papers that compare {technique_A} and {technique_B} in {research_topic}.

4. Find me the most popular papers on {research_topic} published in {specific_year}.

5. Search for interdisciplinary research papers combining {field_A} and {field_B}.

6. I need the key findings and conclusions of the paper titled "{paper_title}".

7. Find me the top 5 papers on {research_topic} based on their impact factor.

8. Search for research papers that discuss the limitations or criticisms of {research_topic}.

9. I need research papers that propose solutions for {problem} in the field of {research_topic}.

10. Find me the latest open access papers on {research_topic} for free download.
