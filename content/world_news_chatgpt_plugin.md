## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Fetch the latest news headlines on the environment or climate change.

2. Provide a summary of the recent news about the technological advancements in {Tech_Field}.

3. Can you give me the latest news from the entertainment industry?

4. I'd like to know the recent developments in space exploration.

5. Summarize the latest news on the current health pandemic.

6. Fetch the latest news about the financial market and economy.

7. Provide a summary of the recent news on the award ceremony {Award_Ceremony_Name}.

8. Can you give me the latest news about the natural disaster {Natural_Disaster_Type} that occurred in {Location}?

9. I'd like to know the recent political developments and election results in {Country_or_Region}.

10. Summarize the latest news on the diplomatic relations between {Country_1} and {Country_2}.
