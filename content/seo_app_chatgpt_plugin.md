## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Analyze my website's content at {url} and suggest improvements for SEO with the keyphrase {keyphrase} in {language} and {location}.

2. Create a meta description optimized for SEO with the keyphrase {keyphrase} in {language} and {location} for the following article: {context}.

3. Generate a list of SEO-optimized subheadings with the keyphrase {keyphrase} in {language} and {location} for an article about {topic}.

4. Rewrite my product description to improve SEO with the keyphrase {keyphrase} in {language} and {location}: {context}.

5. Provide a list of relevant long-tail keywords related to {keyphrase} in {language} and {location} for content creation.

6. Suggest ways to optimize my website's internal linking structure for SEO with the keyphrase {keyphrase} in {language} and {location}.

7. I'd like to improve my website's image SEO for {keyphrase} in {language} and {location}. Please provide tips and examples.

8. Write a SEO-optimized intro paragraph for an article about {topic} with the keyphrase {keyphrase} in {language} and {location}.

9. How do I monitor and track the progress of my SEO efforts using the SEO.app plugin?

10. I have a question about the pricing plans of the SEO.app plugin.
