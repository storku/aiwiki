### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Find me the latest papers on the applications of blockchain technology.

2. I need papers by author A on the topic of astrophysics.

3. Can you find papers on the relationship between nutrition and mental health?

4. I need the most cited papers on the topic of quantum cryptography.

5. Find me the latest papers on the advancements in gene editing techniques.

6. I need papers by author B on the topic of neurodegenerative diseases.

7. Can you find papers on the impact of social media on politics and elections?

8. I need the most cited papers on the topic of evolutionary algorithms.

9. Find me the latest papers on the innovations in virtual reality and augmented reality.

10. I need papers by author C on the topic of human-computer interaction.
