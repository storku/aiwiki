## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Show me the most recent significant earthquakes in the Philippines.

2. Tell me about any earthquakes that happened in the Philippines today.

3. What are the details on the strongest earthquake in the Philippines this week?

4. Can you list the latest earthquakes in the Philippines in the past 24 hours?

5. Find the most recent earthquakes in the Philippines with a magnitude greater than 5.0.

6. I'd like to know about any recent earthquakes in the Philippines along the fault lines.

7. Keep me updated with real-time earthquake notifications in the Philippines.

8. What is the intensity of the latest Philippine earthquake?

9. How many earthquakes have occurred in the Philippines in the past week?

10. Which regions in the Philippines have experienced earthquakes recently?
