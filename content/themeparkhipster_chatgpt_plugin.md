## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. What are the average wait times during weekends for the theme park with the ID of {park_id}?

2. Could you provide the waiting times for family-friendly rides in the theme park with the ID of {park_id}?

3. I'm looking for theme parks with short waiting times today. Can you suggest any parks and their current queue times?

4. What are the fastest roller coasters in the theme park with the ID of {park_id}, and what are their current queue times?

5. How do the current waiting times compare between the theme parks with the IDs of {park_id1} and {park_id2}?

6. Provide me with the queue times for water rides in the theme park with the ID of {park_id}.

7. Which rides have waiting times under 30 minutes in the theme park with the ID of {park_id} right now?

8. Can you list the wait times for all thrill rides in the theme park with the ID of {park_id}?

9. What time of day tends to have the shortest waiting times in the theme park with the ID of {park_id}?

10. I'll be visiting the theme park with the ID of {park_id} next week. What are the typical waiting times I can expect?
