ByteDance Seed** (Chinese: 字节跳动种子团队), also known as the Seed Team, is the [artificial intelligence](/wiki/artificial_intelligence) research division of [ByteDance](/index.php?title=ByteDance&action=edit&redlink=1), established in early 2023 following the success of [OpenAI](/wiki/openai)'s [ChatGPT](/wiki/chatgpt).[&#91;1&#93;](#cite_note-scmp1-1) The division is dedicated to discovering new approaches to [general intelligence](/index.php?title=General_intelligence&action=edit&redlink=1) and pushing the boundaries of AI through foundational research and development of industry-leading AI foundation models.[&#91;2&#93;](#cite_note-seedsite-2)

## History

ByteDance Seed was established in early 2023 as ByteDance's response to the rapid advancement in [large language models](/index.php?title=Large_language_models&action=edit&redlink=1) sparked by [ChatGPT](/wiki/chatgpt)'s release in November 2022.[&#91;1&#93;](#cite_note-scmp1-1) Initially known as the Doubao Seed Team, it was created to develop foundational AI models and explore new approaches to achieving [artificial general intelligence](/wiki/artificial_general_intelligence).[&#91;3&#93;](#cite_note-aibase1-3)

In October 2024, ByteDance established a joint research center with [Tsinghua University](/index.php?title=Tsinghua_University&action=edit&redlink=1)'s Institute for Artificial Intelligence Research (AIR) to advance industry-academia collaboration on large models.[&#91;4&#93;](#cite_note-tsinghua-4)

In January 2025, [Reuters](/index.php?title=Reuters&action=edit&redlink=1) reported that ByteDance planned to spend more than US$12 billion on AI chips and compute infrastructure in 2025, partly via domestic suppliers, aimed at foundation-model training. While not specific to Seed, this investment underpins the company's broader model development efforts.[&#91;5&#93;](#cite_note-reuters-5)

In February 2025, ByteDance underwent a significant restructuring of the Seed division, recruiting [Wu Yonghui](/index.php?title=Wu_Yonghui&action=edit&redlink=1), a former [Google Fellow](/index.php?title=Google_Fellow&action=edit&redlink=1) and [Google DeepMind](/wiki/google_deepmind) Vice President of Research, to lead foundational research efforts.[&#91;6&#93;](#cite_note-scmp2-6) This restructuring came amid competitive pressure from [DeepSeek](/wiki/deepseek), whose models had surpassed ByteDance's [Doubao](/index.php?title=Doubao&action=edit&redlink=1) in daily active users by late January 2025.[&#91;1&#93;](#cite_note-scmp1-1)

In September 2025, the team issued million-level stock options to employees to incentivize performance amid the AI talent war.[&#91;7&#93;](#cite_note-itiger-7)

## Organization and Leadership

The Seed team operates research laboratories in [China](/index.php?title=China&action=edit&redlink=1), [Singapore](/index.php?title=Singapore&action=edit&redlink=1), the [United States](/index.php?title=United_States&action=edit&redlink=1), and other locations globally.[&#91;2&#93;](#cite_note-seedsite-2) The division reports directly to ByteDance CEO [Liang Rubo](/index.php?title=Liang_Rubo&action=edit&redlink=1).[&#91;6&#93;](#cite_note-scmp2-6)

### Key Leadership

- **Wu Yonghui** - Head of Foundational Research (joined February 2025). Wu spent 17 years at [Google](/index.php?title=Google&action=edit&redlink=1), including roles at [Google Brain](/index.php?title=Google_Brain&action=edit&redlink=1) (2014-2023) and as Vice President of Research at [Google DeepMind](/wiki/google_deepmind). He holds a PhD in computer science from the [University of California, Riverside](/index.php?title=University_of_California,_Riverside&action=edit&redlink=1).[&#91;6&#93;](#cite_note-scmp2-6)[&#91;8&#93;](#cite_note-technode-8)

- **Zhu Wenjia** - Head of Applications. Following the 2025 restructuring, Zhu focuses on model application work while remaining part of the Seed department.[&#91;9&#93;](#cite_note-pandaily-9)

- **Li Hang** - Head of Research and Director of AI Lab[&#91;1&#93;](#cite_note-scmp1-1)

- **Xiang Liang** - Head of Applied Machine Learning, led development of [Doubao](/index.php?title=Doubao&action=edit&redlink=1) large language model[&#91;1&#93;](#cite_note-scmp1-1)

- **Huang Wenhao** - Co-founder of [01.ai](/index.php?title=01.ai&action=edit&redlink=1), joined ByteDance in 2024[&#91;1&#93;](#cite_note-scmp1-1)

ByteDance Seed's research spans multiple AI domains:[&#91;2&#93;](#cite_note-seedsite-2)

- [Computer vision](/wiki/computer_vision) and vision-language models

### Vision-Language Models

**[Seed 1.5-VL](/index.php?title=Seed_1.5-VL&action=edit&redlink=1)** is a flagship vision-language foundation model composed of a 532M-parameter vision encoder and a [Mixture of Experts](/wiki/mixture_of_experts) (MoE) LLM with 20B active parameters.[&#91;14&#93;](#cite_note-arxiv-14) Key achievements include:

- State-of-the-art performance on 38 out of 60 public benchmarks[&#91;14&#93;](#cite_note-arxiv-14)

- Superior performance in GUI control and gameplay compared to [OpenAI](/wiki/openai) CUA and [Claude](/wiki/claude) 3.7[&#91;14&#93;](#cite_note-arxiv-14)

- Available via Volcano Engine API (Model ID: doubao-1-5-thinking-vision-pro-250428)[&#91;15&#93;](#cite_note-github-15)

### Video Generation Models

**[Seedance 1.0](/index.php?title=Seedance_1.0&action=edit&redlink=1)** is a multi-task video generation model capable of:[&#91;16&#93;](#cite_note-seedance-16)

- Creating 1080p videos with smooth motion and cinematic aesthetics

- Native multi-shot storytelling with consistent visual style across transitions

- Text-to-video and image-to-video generation

- Support for diverse stylistic expressions from photorealism to animation

### Specialized Models

- **[Seed-Prover](/index.php?title=Seed-Prover&action=edit&redlink=1)** - Lemma-style whole-proof reasoning model for mathematical theorem proving, achieving 78.1% success on formalized IMO problems, solved 5 out of 6 problems in the 2025 International Mathematical Olympiad (IMO) Grand Challenge[&#91;10&#93;](#cite_note-seedresearch-10)[&#91;22&#93;](#cite_note-medium-22)

- **[BFS-Prover](/index.php?title=BFS-Prover&action=edit&redlink=1)** - Theorem proving LLMs with open models at 7B and 32B parameters (V1-7B, V2-7B/32B), targeting verifiable formal proofs in Lean and lemma-first reasoning strategies[&#91;23&#93;](#cite_note-huggingface2-23)

- **[Seed-LiveInterpret 2.0](/index.php?title=Seed-LiveInterpret_2.0&action=edit&redlink=1)** - End-to-end simultaneous interpretation model with ultra-low latency and voice cloning capabilities[&#91;10&#93;](#cite_note-seedresearch-10)

- **[BAGEL](/index.php?title=BAGEL&action=edit&redlink=1)** - Open-source foundational model supporting multimodal understanding and generation of texts, images, and videos[&#91;2&#93;](#cite_note-seedsite-2)

- **[UI-TARS-1.5](/index.php?title=UI-TARS-1.5&action=edit&redlink=1)** - Open-source multimodal agent for diverse tasks in virtual environments[&#91;2&#93;](#cite_note-seedsite-2)

- **[Seed Music](/index.php?title=Seed_Music&action=edit&redlink=1)** - AI music and audio generation model for generative audio capabilities[&#91;10&#93;](#cite_note-seedresearch-10)

- **[Sa2VA](/index.php?title=Sa2VA&action=edit&redlink=1)** - Model marrying SAM2 with LLaVA for dense grounded understanding of images and videos[&#91;24&#93;](#cite_note-huggingface3-24)

## Applications and Platforms

ByteDance Seed's models power over 50 real-world applications,[&#91;25&#93;](#cite_note-github2-25) including:

- **[Doubao](/index.php?title=Doubao&action=edit&redlink=1)** (豆包) - ByteDance's flagship AI chatbot and assistant platform in China

- **[Coze](/index.php?title=Coze&action=edit&redlink=1)** - AI chatbot and application development platform for creating custom AI agents

- **[Jimeng](/index.php?title=Jimeng&action=edit&redlink=1)** (即梦) - Text-to-image and video generation application launched in 2024[&#91;26&#93;](#cite_note-scmp4-26)

- **[Volcano Engine](/index.php?title=Volcano_Engine&action=edit&redlink=1)** (Volcengine) - ByteDance's enterprise cloud platform providing API access to Seed models

- **[Feishu](/index.php?title=Feishu&action=edit&redlink=1)/[Lark](/index.php?title=Lark&action=edit&redlink=1)** - Enterprise collaboration platforms

- **[CapCut](/index.php?title=CapCut&action=edit&redlink=1)** - Video editing application

## Research Programs

### Seed Edge Research Program

The Seed Edge Research Program focuses on exploring the frontiers of intelligence and tackling long-term research challenges in AI toward establishing general intelligence.[&#91;27&#93;](#cite_note-seededge-27)

### Top Seed Talent Program

An initiative to attract and develop top AI research talent globally, recruiting top PhDs and interns from universities.[&#91;28&#93;](#cite_note-topseed-28)

## Competition and Market Position

ByteDance Seed operates in a highly competitive AI landscape, facing competition from:

- Domestic Chinese competitors: [DeepSeek](/wiki/deepseek), [Baidu](/index.php?title=Baidu&action=edit&redlink=1), [Alibaba](/index.php?title=Alibaba&action=edit&redlink=1), [Tencent](/index.php?title=Tencent&action=edit&redlink=1)

- International competitors: [OpenAI](/wiki/openai), [Google DeepMind](/wiki/google_deepmind), [Anthropic](/wiki/anthropic)

The division's restructuring in February 2025 was partly attributed to competitive pressure from [DeepSeek](/wiki/deepseek), whose reasoning models had gained significant market share in China.[&#91;1&#93;](#cite_note-scmp1-1) ByteDance CEO Liang Rubo acknowledged that the company had been slow to follow up on technical directions like [chain-of-thought](/index.php?title=Chain-of-thought&action=edit&redlink=1) reasoning after [OpenAI](/wiki/openai)'s o1 model release.[&#91;1&#93;](#cite_note-scmp1-1)

## Collaborations

ByteDance Seed actively collaborates with academic and industry partners. The joint research center with [Tsinghua University](/index.php?title=Tsinghua_University&action=edit&redlink=1)'s AIR focuses on advancing large model technologies through shared resources and expertise.[&#91;4&#93;](#cite_note-tsinghua-4) The division has also established partnerships with various institutions globally as part of its talent development and research initiatives.
