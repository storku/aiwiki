## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Find the best deal on a 55-inch 4K Smart TV from top online shops.

2. Compare prices of a GoPro HERO9 Black across multiple online retailers.

3. What online stores offer the lowest price for a PlayStation 5 gaming console?

4. Search for Apple AirPods Pro and display the prices from different shops.

5. Identify the most affordable Windows laptop with an i7 processor and at least 16GB RAM from online stores.

6. Look for discounted prices on Dyson V11 vacuum cleaners in online shops.

7. Find the best online store to buy a Garmin Forerunner 245 smartwatch at the lowest price.

8. Compare prices for a Nikon D5600 DSLR camera with 18-55mm VR lens from different online retailers.

9. Search for the cheapest online deals for an Instant Pot Duo Plus 6 Qt multi-cooker.

10. Locate the lowest price for an iPad Pro 11-inch, 256GB, Wi-Fi model from various online shops.
