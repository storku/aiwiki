**Ai Tool Hunt** is a [Plugin](/wiki/plugin) for [ChatGPT](/wiki/chatgpt).              

## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. What are the top 3 AI tools for {specific task or problem} that have a user-friendly interface?

2. Can you recommend AI tools that can assist in {specific task or problem} and offer live customer support?

3. I'm looking for AI tools that can help with {specific task or problem} and are GDPR compliant. Can you find them?

4. Find AI tools that can solve {specific task or problem} and have a strong community behind them.

5. What are the AI tools for {specific task or problem} that can easily integrate with {specific tool or API}?

6. Can you suggest AI tools that can assist in {specific task or problem} and provide detailed documentation for users?

7. I need AI tools that can help with {specific task or problem}, focusing on the applications in the field of {specific sector or industry}. Can you find them?

8. What are the AI tools for {specific task or problem} that have received awards or recognition in the past two years?

9. Can you recommend AI tools that can help with {specific task or problem} and are optimized for small businesses?

10. What are the AI tools for {specific task or problem} that have an extensive library of pre-built models or templates?
