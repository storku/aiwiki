## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. What's the UV index for [City Name] today?

2. Are there any weather alerts or warnings in [City Name] at the moment?

3. What's the chance of snowfall in [City Name] this week?

4. What's the sea temperature near [City Name] right now?

5. How strong is the wind in [City Name] today, and what direction is it coming from?

6. What's the air quality index (AQI) status for [City Name] today?

7. What's the coldest temperature ever recorded in [City Name]?

8. How is the pollen count in [City Name] today?

9. What's the expected precipitation for [City Name] in the next 48 hours?

10. Is there a possibility of thunderstorms in [City Name] today?
