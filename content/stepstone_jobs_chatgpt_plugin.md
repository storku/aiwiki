## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Find Jobs for Recent Graduates: "Search for job opportunities for recent graduates in Cologne.

2. Search for Internships: "Find internship opportunities in Berlin in the tech industry.

3. Find Jobs Requiring Language Skills: "Search for jobs in Munich that require proficiency in both German and English.

4. Search for Remote Jobs: "Find full-time remote job opportunities located in Germany.

5. Find Jobs with a Specific Technology: "Search for jobs in Hamburg that require expertise in Python programming.

6. Search for Jobs by Contract Length: "Find temporary jobs in Nuremberg lasting at least 6 months.

7. Find Jobs for a Specific Experience Level: "Search for mid-level management positions in Dresden.

8. Find Jobs with Specific Work Hours: "Search for part-time jobs in Leipzig with flexible working hours.

9. Find Jobs in a Specific Sector: "Search for jobs in the renewable energy sector in Bremen.

10. Find Start-up Jobs: "Search for job opportunities in start-ups located in Berlin.
