## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. What are the current trends in the {industry} sector?

2. Any recent news regarding mergers and acquisitions?

3. Can you find the latest news about {person_name} in the business world?

4. What are the top headlines in the global economy today?

5. Find the most recent updates on {country_name}'s economy.

6. Can you provide the latest news about cryptocurrency and blockchain?

7. What are the most significant financial events reported this week?

8. What is the latest news on any major IPOs in the market?

9. Can you find the latest news about {company_name}'s financial performance?

10. Provide the latest news on technology companies and their business developments.
