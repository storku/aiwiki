## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Calculate the score of my cribbage hand with the starter card as 8 of Spades and my hand consisting of 9 of Hearts, 10 of Diamonds, Jack of Clubs, and Queen of Hearts.

2. Score my cribbage hand with the starter card as Ace of Diamonds and my hand as 2 of Hearts, 3 of Spades, 4 of Clubs, and 5 of Diamonds.

3. What's the score of my cribbage hand if the starter card is 5 of Hearts and my hand is 6 of Diamonds, 7 of Spades, 8 of Clubs, and 9 of Hearts?

4. Score my cribbage hand with the starter card as 10 of Spades and my hand consisting of Jack of Hearts, Queen of Clubs, King of Spades, and Ace of Clubs.

5. How many points do I get in cribbage if the starter card is 3 of Clubs and my hand is 4 of Hearts, 5 of Diamonds, 6 of Spades, and 7 of Hearts?

6. What's the score of my cribbage hand if the starter card is Jack of Diamonds and my hand is 9 of Hearts, 10 of Spades, Queen of Clubs, and King of Diamonds?

7. Calculate the score of my cribbage hand with the starter card as 6 of Spades and my hand consisting of 7 of Hearts, 8 of Diamonds, 9 of Clubs, and 10 of Hearts.

8. Score my cribbage hand with the starter card as King of Spades and my hand as 10 of Hearts, Jack of Diamonds, Queen of Clubs, and Ace of Spades.

9. What's the score of my cribbage hand if the starter card is 2 of Hearts and my hand is 2 of Diamonds, 2 of Spades, 2 of Clubs, and 7 of Hearts?

10. How many points do I get in cribbage if the starter card is 9 of Clubs and my hand is Ace of Hearts, 2 of Diamonds, 3 of Spades, and 4 of Hearts?
