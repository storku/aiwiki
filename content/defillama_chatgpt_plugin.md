**Defillama** is a [Plugin](/wiki/plugin) for [ChatGPT](/wiki/chatgpt).              

## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Retrieve the top 5 DeFi protocols based on Total Value Locked (TVL) in the last 24 hours.

2. Get the top 10 DeFi protocols based on their 7-day percentage change in TVL.

3. Retrieve the TVL of the specific token (token_name) listed on protocol_defillamaId protocol.

4. Compare the TVL of protocol_A_defillamaId and protocol_B_defillamaId over the last 30 days.

5. Get the top 5 DeFi tokens that gained the most value on chain_name chain in the last week.

6. Retrieve the overall TVL metric for a specific category, such as lending or DEX (category_name).

7. Get the top 10 protocols with the highest average daily trading volume in the last month.

8. Retrieve the blockchain or L2 with the highest growth in TVL over the past week.

9. Get the top 5 DeFi protocols with the highest number of unique users in the last 30 days.

10. Retrieve the list of top-performing assets on the protocol_defillamaId protocol in the last week.
