## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Roll a Fate die and let me know the result, please.

2. I need to roll 7 six-sided dice for my character's intelligence attribute.

3. Can you roll 3 twelve-sided dice for my stamina regeneration?

4. Roll a 50-sided die for my custom tabletop game.

5. I need to roll a combination of 2 eight-sided dice and 3 ten-sided dice for my skill check.

6. Roll 6 four-sided dice and sum the three highest values for my character's wisdom attribute.

7. Can you roll a 30-sided die for this rare spellcasting attempt?

8. I need to roll 1 twenty-sided die and 1 six-sided die and add them together for my attack roll.

9. Roll 5 ten-sided dice and drop the two lowest for my character's charisma attribute.

10. Can you roll a 4-sided die? I'm trying to decide which path to take in this adventure.
