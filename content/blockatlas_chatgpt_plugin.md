## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Find the population density of {city_name}, {state_name}.

2. Get the data for life expectancy in {state_name}.

3. Retrieve the ethnicity distribution in {county_name}, {state_name}.

4. Fetch the data for the number of veterans in {city_name}, {state_name}.

5. Provide the data for the percentage of foreign-born residents in {state_name}.

6. Get the data for the total number of households in {county_name}, {state_name}.

7. Retrieve the data for the commute time statistics in {city_name}, {state_name}.

8. Fetch the data for the percentage of people with a high school diploma in {state_name}.

9. Provide the data for the number of registered voters in {county_name}, {state_name}.

10. Get the data for the median rent price in {city_name}, {state_name}.
