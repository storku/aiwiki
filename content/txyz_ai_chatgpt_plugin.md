## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Could you provide a short summary of the research paper with the arXiv ID arxiv_id?

2. I'd like a quick explanation of the main points of the paper with the arXiv ID arxiv_id.

3. Give me an overview of the scientific paper with the arXiv ID arxiv_id.

4. What are the main findings of the research paper with the arXiv ID arxiv_id?

5. Can you help me understand the main conclusions of the paper with the arXiv ID arxiv_id?

6. Provide a brief assessment of the research paper with the arXiv ID arxiv_id.

7. I'm curious about the paper with the arXiv ID arxiv_id. Can you give a short summary?

8. Offer a brief analysis of the paper with the arXiv ID arxiv_id.

9. What's the main contribution of the research paper with the arXiv ID arxiv_id?

10. Help me grasp the major points of the scientific paper with the arXiv ID arxiv_id.
