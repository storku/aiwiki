## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Analyze the CPC (Cost Per Click) for the keywords {keyword1}, {keyword2}, {keyword3} in {language} and {location}.

2. Estimate the organic traffic potential for the website {website} in {language} and {location}.

3. Identify the top 10 competitors for the keyword {keyword} in {language} and {location}.

4. Provide the backlink profile for the website {website} in {language} and {location}.

5. Determine the top landing pages for the website {website} in {language} and {location}, based on traffic.

6. Recommend optimization strategies for the website {website} to rank higher for the keyword {keyword} in {language} and {location}.

7. Evaluate the keyword gap between websites {website1} and {website2} in {language} and {location}.

8. Discover the popular topics related to the keyword {keyword} in {language} and {location} for content ideas.

9. Examine the click-through rate (CTR) for the keyword {keyword} in {language} and {location} on a {device}.

10. Assess the domain authority of the website {website} in {language} and {location}.
