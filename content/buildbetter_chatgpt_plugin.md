## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Pull up the transcripts of all project review meetings where 'deadline adjustments' were discussed.

2. Find the most recent sales team calls where 'prospecting strategies' were a key topic.

3. Retrieve the last 5 internal meetings where 'employee engagement initiatives' were discussed.

4. Show me all calls involving 'team collaboration tools' as a focal point in the last two months.

5. Find the call transcripts where 'marketing campaign results' were reviewed in the past 30 days.

6. Pull up the transcripts of all calls with 'client feedback' shared within the last six months.

7. Show me the discussions from the most recent 'quarterly earnings call' that took place.

8. Retrieve the transcripts of all calls focused on 'budget allocation' for the upcoming fiscal year.

9. Find all calls where 'partnerships and collaborations' were discussed by the executive team in the past two quarters.

10. Show me the top 3 customer support calls where 'data privacy concerns' were raised.
