## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Create a resume for me to apply for an internship. Here's my information: insert details here.

2. I need a resume for a career change. Here are my details: insert details here.

3. Help me create a resume for a freelancer position. Here's my information: insert details here.

4. I'm applying for a remote job. Can you help me create a resume? Here are my details: insert details here.

5. I need a resume tailored for an entry-level job. Here's my information: insert details here.

6. Create a resume for a management position. Here's my personal and professional information: insert details here.

7. I need a resume for a graduate program application. Here are my details: insert details here.

8. Generate a resume for me highlighting my volunteer experience. Here's my information: insert details here.

9. I'm applying for a research assistant position. Can you help me create a resume? Here are my details: insert details here.

10. I need a resume focusing on my transferable skills. Here's my information: insert details here.
