## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Provide me with a sample of MBTI test questions to determine my personality type.

2. I'm interested in taking the MBTI assessment. Help me understand which questions to answer.

3. What are some typical questions on the MBTI test? Generate a list for me.

4. Can you guide me through the process of taking an MBTI test? Begin with a few questions.

5. Help me discover my MBTI type by giving me a series of test questions to answer.

6. Administer a mini MBTI test for me to find my personality type.

7. I'm curious about my MBTI personality type. Can you generate some questions for me to answer?

8. Prepare a list of MBTI assessment questions for me to complete and analyze my personality type.

9. Provide me with a selection of MBTI questions that will help me understand my personality type better.

10. Assist me in taking an MBTI test by giving me questions that reveal my type.
