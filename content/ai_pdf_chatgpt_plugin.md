## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Can you identify the author and title of the PDF at this URL: {pdf_url}?

2. What are the most important statistics mentioned in the PDF at this URL: {pdf_url}?

3. Can you find any case studies or examples in the PDF at this URL: {pdf_url} related to {query}?

4. What is the methodology used in the PDF at this URL: {pdf_url}?

5. What are the limitations or criticisms of the PDF at this URL: {pdf_url}?

6. Can you provide a list of key terms or concepts mentioned in the PDF at this URL: {pdf_url}?

7. What are the main recommendations or implications in the PDF at this URL: {pdf_url}?

8. Can you provide a comparison of the topics discussed in the PDF at this URL: {pdf_url} and at this URL: {second_pdf_url}?

9. Who is the target audience for the PDF at this URL: {pdf_url}?

10. What is the historical context of the PDF at this URL: {pdf_url}?
