## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Can you summarize the main arguments presented in this YouTube video: {YouTube URL}?

2. I need a brief overview of the key information on this webpage, especially anything related to {specific subject}: {Webpage URL}.

3. Please provide a summary of the main conclusions from this PDF document: {PDF URL}.

4. Summarize the introduction and conclusion sections of this PDF document for me: {PDF URL}.

5. This webpage has a lot of information. Can you give me the most important takeaways: {Webpage URL}?

6. I'm interested in the core concepts of this YouTube video. Can you provide a quick summary: {YouTube URL}?

7. Please give a summary of the key ideas discussed from minute 5 to 10 in this YouTube video: {YouTube URL}.

8. Summarize the main findings or recommendations from this webpage: {Webpage URL}.

9. Can you help me understand the main points of this PDF document by summarizing pages 1 to 5: {PDF URL}?

10. I need a summary of the key information in this web article, focusing on {specific theme or subject}: {Webpage URL}.
