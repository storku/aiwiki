## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Recommend the top-quality {product_name} on Amazon.

2. What are the best value-for-money {product_name} on Amazon?

3. Find me a well-reviewed {product_name} on Amazon.

4. Show me the most durable {product_name} on Amazon.

5. Can you find the best-rated {product_name} under $50 on Amazon?

6. Search for the top 3 {product_name} on Amazon.

7. Recommend the best budget {product_name} on Amazon.

8. What are the best eco-friendly {product_name} available on Amazon?

9. Find me the best beginner-friendly {product_name} on Amazon.

10. Show me the top-performing {product_name} on Amazon.
