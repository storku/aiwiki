## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. What are the current regional rankings for the top football teams?

2. Who are the top scorers in the {league name} this season?

3. Can you give me updates on the latest transfer news for {team name}?

4. When is the next match between {team1 name} and {team2 name} in {league name}?

5. Who are the referees officiating the upcoming match between {team1 name} and {team2 name}?

6. Which stadium will host the upcoming match between {team1 name} and {team2 name}?

7. What are the past scores and results for the head-to-head matches between {team1 name} and {team2 name}?

8. Which players are included in the starting lineup for the upcoming match of {team name}?

9. Who is the current manager for {team name}?

10. What's the weather forecast for the match between {team1 name} and {team2 name} on {date}?
