### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Find me a 3-bedroom house in {city} with a swimming pool.

2. Can you find me an apartment in {city} with included utilities and a maximum rent of {price}?

3. I'm looking for a loft with a rooftop terrace in {city}.

4. I need a townhouse in {city} with a finished basement.

5. Find me a rental property in {city} near public transportation.

6. I'm looking for a cat-friendly apartment in {city} with an elevator.

7. Can you find me a property in {city} with a view and a private balcony?

8. I need a wheelchair-accessible apartment in {city} with ground floor entry.

9. Find me a short-term rental in {city} with a flexible lease.

10. I'm looking for a furnished room to rent in {city} with a private bathroom.
