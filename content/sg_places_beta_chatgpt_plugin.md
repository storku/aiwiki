## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Recommend the top 5 attractions in Singapore for families with young children.

2. Find vegan or vegetarian restaurants in Singapore near [your_location_here].

3. Show me budget-friendly hotels in Singapore within walking distance of MRT stations.

4. Identify art galleries and museums in Singapore worth visiting.

5. Locate shopping malls in Singapore with a wide range of fashion stores.

6. Are there any food festivals or culinary events in Singapore during [your_desired_month_here]?

7. Suggest romantic getaway accommodations in Singapore for a weekend stay.

8. Plan a 3-day itinerary in Singapore that includes must-see attractions and local experiences.

9. Find wheelchair-accessible attractions and facilities in Singapore for people with disabilities.

10. Show me available guided walking tours in Singapore that explore historic districts.
