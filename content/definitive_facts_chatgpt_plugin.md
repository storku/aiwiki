## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. What is the current inflation rate in ${COUNTRY_NAME}?

2. Which country has the highest number of ${ANIMAL_NAME} in the wild?

3. What are the top 3 highest mountains in ${CONTINENT_NAME}?

4. Who held the world record for the ${SPORTING_EVENT_NAME} in ${YEAR}?

5. What is the percentage of renewable energy sources used in ${COUNTRY_NAME}?

6. What was the average price of gasoline in ${COUNTRY_NAME} in ${YEAR}?

7. Who were the top 5 richest people in the world in ${YEAR}?

8. What is the most popular tourist destination in ${COUNTRY_NAME}?

9. How many gold medals did ${COUNTRY_NAME} win in the ${YEAR} Olympics?

10. What is the highest-grossing video game of all time?
