## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Please find me systematic reviews on {topic} from the past decade.

2. Can you provide a list of authors who frequently publish papers on {topic}?

3. Find me the latest conference proceedings related to {topic}.

4. I need a summary of the latest breakthrough findings in the field of {topic}.

5. How many papers have been published on {topic} in the last two years?

6. Can you find open access papers on {topic} for me to read?

7. Please show me the top five authors with the most citations on {topic}.

8. Give me a list of journals with the highest impact factor in the field of {topic}.

9. Find me the most popular research methodologies used in studies on {topic}.

10. I need a list of key terms and concepts in the field of {topic}. Can you provide it?
