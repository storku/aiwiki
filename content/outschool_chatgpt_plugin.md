### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Search for a weekend workshop on digital art for my 14-year-old in the Eastern Standard Time Zone.

2. Find a marine biology course for my 15-year-old in the Central Time Zone, starting in the next month.

3. Look for an affordable theater class for my 10-year-old in the Greenwich Mean Time Zone.

4. Show me beginner-friendly dance classes for a 12-year-old in the Pacific Standard Time Zone.

5. Search for history lessons suitable for a group of 13-year-olds in the Mountain Time Zone.

6. Find a course on financial literacy for a 17-year-old starting in September, in the Australian Central Time Zone.

7. Look for a summer camp focused on game design for my 14-year-old in the Philippine Time Zone.

8. Search for engaging public speaking classes for a 16-year-old in the Central European Time Zone.

9. Show me diverse language courses for a 13-year-old in the Eastern African Time Zone.

10. Find online classes on filmmaking for my 18-year-old in the Brazilian Standard Time Zone.
