## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Upload this photo for editing and apply a sepia filter: {photo_url}.

2. Increase the brightness of the photo with ID {photo_id} by 20%.

3. Resize the uploaded photo with ID {photo_id} to a width of 1440 pixels and maintain its aspect ratio.

4. Add a watermark to the photo with ID {photo_id} using the text "Copyright © {year}".

5. Adjust the contrast of the photo with ID {photo_id} by 15% and the saturation by 10%.

6. Upload this photo for editing, and flip it horizontally: {photo_url}.

7. Merge the photos with IDs {photo_id1} and {photo_id2} side by side, maintaining their respective aspect ratios.

8. Apply a black and white filter to the photo with ID {photo_id} and increase its sharpness by 25%.

9. Upload this photo for editing, resize it to a height of 1080 pixels while maintaining its aspect ratio, and then crop it to a width of 1920 pixels: {photo_url}.

10. Apply a vignette effect to the photo with ID {photo_id} with a radius of 250 pixels and a strength of 75%.
