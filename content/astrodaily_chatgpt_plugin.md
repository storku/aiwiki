## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Search for NASA images of famous nebulas.

2. Show me a collection of NASA images of Earth from space.

3. Find images from the James Webb Space Telescope.

4. Search for images of the dwarf planet Pluto taken by NASA.

5. Find and display NASA images related to solar flares.

6. Show me NASA images of the process of astronaut training.

7. Search for images of various exoplanets discovered using NASA telescopes.

8. Find images from the SpaceX Crew Dragon missions with NASA.

9. Show me a collection of images from the NASA Curiosity Rover.

10. Search for images related to black holes in the NASA database.
