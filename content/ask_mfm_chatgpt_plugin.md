## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. What opinions do the hosts of the My First Million podcast have on {TOPIC}?

2. What valuable lessons can I learn from the My First Million podcast about {TOPIC}?

3. How do the hosts of the My First Million podcast approach {TOPIC}?

4. Are there any episodes of My First Million podcast specifically focusing on {TOPIC}?

5. What tips and suggestions do the My First Million podcast hosts share about {TOPIC}?

6. Has the My First Million podcast recently discussed {TOPIC}? What were their key takeaways?

7. What experiences do the My First Million podcast hosts share about {TOPIC}?

8. Does the My First Million podcast offer any strategies or recommendations about {TOPIC}?

9. What are some notable episodes or discussions from the My First Million podcast related to {TOPIC}?

10. Have the My First Million podcast hosts interviewed any prominent figures in regards to {TOPIC}?
