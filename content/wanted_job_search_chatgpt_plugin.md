## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Find me job positions related to {keyword} in various countries.

2. Search for international job opportunities in the {keyword} field.

3. What are some global job opportunities involving {keyword}?

4. Show me {keyword}-related job openings around the world.

5. Can you help me find worldwide job positions in the {keyword} sector?

6. I'm interested in international jobs within the {keyword} industry. Can you find some?

7. Can you provide me with global job listings for {keyword} professionals?

8. I am looking for {keyword} job positions in different countries. Can you assist?

9. Search for global job vacancies that require expertise in {keyword}.

10. Help me discover worldwide job openings related to {keyword}.
