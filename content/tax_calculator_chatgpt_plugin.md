## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. What is the sales tax amount for a purchase of {transactionValue} in {city}, {state}?

2. Can you help me determine the sales tax for a transaction worth {transactionValue} in {city}, {state}?

3. I'd like to know the sales tax for a {transactionValue} purchase in {city}, {state}.

4. Please calculate the sales tax on a transaction of {transactionValue} in {city}, {state}.

5. What is the applicable sales tax for a transaction of {transactionValue} in {city}, {state}?

6. I'm making a purchase worth {transactionValue} in {city}, {state}. Can you calculate the sales tax for me?

7. Please compute the sales tax for a transaction of {transactionValue} in {city}, {state}.

8. For a purchase of {transactionValue} in {city}, {state}, what would the sales tax be?

9. Calculate the sales tax amount for a {transactionValue} transaction in {city}, {state}.

10. How much sales tax should I pay for a purchase of {transactionValue} in {city}, {state}?
