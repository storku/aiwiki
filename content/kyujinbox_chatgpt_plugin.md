## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Find me remote work opportunities in the gaming industry based in Japan.

2. Search for entry-level engineer positions in renewable energy within the Hiroshima area.

3. Show me the top 15 cybersecurity job openings in Tokyo on a part-time basis.

4. I'm looking for IT consultancy jobs in Okinawa. Can you find some for me?

5. Search for internship opportunities in the fashion industry located in Kyoto.

6. I'm looking for full-time positions in the hotel and hospitality industry in Osaka. Can you help me find some?

7. Find me part-time jobs in the human resources department of companies based in Nagasaki.

8. I'm interested in seasonal work opportunities in the tourism sector in Hokkaido. Can you find some for me?

9. Search for full-time research-based positions in the biotechnology field in Kawasaki.

10. I'm looking for contract-based content writer jobs in the media industry in Kumamoto. Can you find some for me?
