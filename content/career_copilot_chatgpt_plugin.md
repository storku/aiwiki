## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Locate ${title} job opportunities in ${location} that focus on ${skills}.

2. Find me recent job postings for a ${title} in ${location} with a heavy emphasis on ${skills}.

3. Discover ${title} positions in ${location} that need proficiency in ${skills}.

4. Please list current ${title} job openings within ${location} seeking candidates with ${skills}.

5. I want to see ${title} job postings in ${location} that highlight ${skills}.

6. Uncover remote ${title} jobs that demand knowledge of ${skills}.

7. Help me find ${title} job positions in ${location} seeking individuals skilled in ${skills}.

8. Search for emerging ${title} job vacancies in ${location} with a focus on ${skills}.

9. Identify job listings for ${title} roles in ${location} that require expertise in ${skills}.

10. Please provide available ${title} job options in ${location} that require mastery of ${skills}.
