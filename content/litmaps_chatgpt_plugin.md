**Litmaps** is a [Plugin](/wiki/plugin) for [ChatGPT](/wiki/chatgpt).              

## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Find articles related to the topic {topic} on Litmaps.

2. Retrieve the top 5 most cited articles within the search results.

3. Generate a mindmap based on the articles with IDs {article_ids}.

4. Extract the most common keywords among the chosen articles.

5. Get me a list of the 10 most recent articles related to {keyword}.

6. Present a summary of the main findings for the article ID {article_id}.

7. Compare the top 3 articles based on their relevance to {topic}.

8. Identify any gaps in the literature related to the keyword {keyword}.

9. Get me the search results for the search type 'expanded'.

10. Fetch me a list of similar papers to the article with ID {article_id}.
