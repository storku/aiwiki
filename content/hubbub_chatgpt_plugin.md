## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Pregnancy and Health Risks: "What are the health risks and safety precautions for pregnant women in {city_name}?

2. Health Guidance for Public Events: "Provide safety guidance for organizing public events in {city_name}.

3. Childcare Safety Recommendations: "What safety measures should childcare centers in {city_name} follow?

4. Travel Advisory: "What is the current travel advisory for {city_name} regarding health risks?

5. COVID-19 Vaccination Updates: "Get the latest COVID-19 vaccination updates for {city_name}.

6. Workplace Safety Measures: "What safety measures should be implemented in workplaces within {city_name}?

7. Guidance for Visiting Parks: "Provide safety guidance for visiting parks and recreational areas in {city_name}.

8. Senior Citizen Health Risks: "What are the specific health risks for senior citizens living in {city_name}?

9. Public Transportation Safety Guidelines: "What precautions should be taken while using public transportation in {city_name}?

10. Healthcare Facility Risks: "What is the potential risk of healthcare-associated infections in {city_name}?
