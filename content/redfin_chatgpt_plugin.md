## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Find homes in ${city}, ${state} within ${distance} miles of public transportation.

2. Search for properties in ${city}, ${state} with a walk score of at least ${walkScoreMin}.

3. Show me homes for sale in ${city}, ${state} within ${schoolDistrict} school district.

4. Find new construction homes in ${city}, ${state} that will be completed within ${completionMonthsMax} months.

5. Search for multi-family properties for sale in ${city}, ${state} with at least ${unitsMin} units.

6. Find homes in ${city}, ${state} that were built within the last ${builtInLastYearsMax} years.

7. Show me properties in ${city}, ${state} with energy-efficient features.

8. Find homes for sale in ${city}, ${state} with ${garageMin} or more garage spaces.

9. Search for pet-friendly condos for sale in ${city}, ${state}.

10. Provide information about the current housing market for ${city}, ${state}.
