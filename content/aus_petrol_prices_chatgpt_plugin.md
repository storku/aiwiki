## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. What is the current average petrol price in New South Wales?

2. Tell me the cheapest petrol price today in Adelaide.

3. List the top 5 petrol stations in Perth with the lowest petrol prices today.

4. How does the current average petrol price in Melbourne compare to a year ago?

5. Show me a graph of weekly average petrol prices in Sydney over the past 6 months.

6. What was the average petrol price last Friday in Brisbane?

7. Predict the average petrol price in Canberra for next week, based on historical data.

8. What is the difference in average petrol prices today between Hobart and Darwin?

9. Display a heatmap of petrol prices for petrol stations in the Gold Coast region today.

10. What are the top 3 fuel brands with the lowest petrol prices in Sydney today?
