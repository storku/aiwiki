## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Summarize the key points of the podcast episode titled episode_title.

2. List the main topics discussed in the show show_name.

3. Find podcast episodes related to the topic topic_name.

4. Summarize the central arguments of the debate on show show_name episode episode_number.

5. Identify podcast episodes where the guest guest_name appeared.

6. What are the most popular episodes of the show show_name?

7. Provide a brief overview of the YouTube video with the id video_id.

8. Recommend similar podcast episodes/shows based on show_name.

9. Highlight the most interesting moments in the show show_name.

10. Locate podcast episodes discussing the topic topic_name from the show show_name.
