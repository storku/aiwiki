## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Search for entry-level software developer jobs in Seattle with a minimum salary of $65,000 per year.

2. Find me part-time graphic design positions in Houston, Texas that were posted in the last 14 days.

3. Show me the available job opportunities at Apple in Cupertino within a 25-mile commute of my location.

4. I'm interested in cybersecurity positions in Washington D.C. that offer remote work and pay at least $85,000 per year.

5. Find sales representative roles in Atlanta, Georgia with a maximum salary of $50,000 plus commissions.

6. Look for environmental jobs in Denver, Colorado that pay at least $45,000 per year and start within the next 60 days.

7. I'm looking for full-time human resources positions in San Diego, California within a 15-mile commute of my location.

8. Search for internships in the entertainment industry in Los Angeles with a duration of 3-6 months.

9. Find me job openings in the pharmaceutical industry in Philadelphia that require a master's degree and pay at least $75,000 per year.

10. Can you find job opportunities in the renewable energy sector in Portland, Oregon that offer relocation assistance?
