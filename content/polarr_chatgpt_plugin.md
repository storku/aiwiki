## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Can you recommend a filter to enhance the blue tones in my beach photos?

2. Which filter would you suggest for adding a warm, cozy feel to my indoor images?

3. Find me a filter that can create a moody atmosphere in my street photography.

4. What is a good filter for enhancing the details in nature and wildlife photography?

5. I'm in search of a filter that will make my cityscape photos more vibrant and eye-catching.

6. Can you suggest a filter that can add a subtle vintage effect to my pictures?

7. What filter would work best for creating a soft, pastel look in my photos?

8. I'd like a filter recommendation for improving the contrast and texture in my architectural shots.

9. Which filter can help me achieve a dramatic and high-energy look for sports photography?

10. Find a filter that can mimic the look and feel of film photography for my images.
