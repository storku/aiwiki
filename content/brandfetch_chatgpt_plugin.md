## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Can you provide the contact information for the company associated with the domain {domain_name}?

2. Determine the slogan or tagline of the brand with the domain {domain_name}.

3. What is the target audience for the brand with the domain {domain_name}?

4. Fetch the brand history or company background for the domain {domain_name}.

5. What is the mission or vision statement of the company or brand with the domain {domain_name}?

6. Can you find the social media presence of the brand associated with the domain {domain_name}?

7. Identify any known collaborations or partnerships for the brand with the domain {domain_name}.

8. What are the primary products or services offered by the brand with the domain {domain_name}?

9. Determine the brand's market position or industry ranking for the domain {domain_name}.

10. Find the key competitors or rival brands for the domain {domain_name}.
