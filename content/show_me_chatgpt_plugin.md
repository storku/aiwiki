## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Can you create a Venn diagram comparing plant and animal cells?

2. Show me an infographic of the causes and effects of climate change.

3. Draw a diagram explaining the components of a computer motherboard.

4. Illustrate the Krebs cycle in a simple diagram.

5. Can you show me a flowchart of a customer complaint handling process?

6. I'd like to see a diagram of the stages of human fetal development.

7. Can you create a diagram demonstrating how a smartphone works?

8. Show me a visual representation of Maslow's Hierarchy of Needs.

9. I need a diagram illustrating the structure of a eukaryotic cell.

10. Create a timeline highlighting the key events in the history of the internet.
