## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Design a satisfaction survey for my newly launched product, {Product_Name}.

2. Create an evaluation form for my training session on {Session_Topic}.

3. Construct a payment form for my online course, {Course_Name}.

4. Generate a personality quiz for my website visitors based on {Personality_Traits}.

5. Can you make a pre-employment questionnaire for applicants at my company, {Company_Name}?

6. I'd like a membership application form for my club, {Club_Name}.

7. Produce a testimonial submission form for my clients, {Business_Name}.

8. Create a donation form for my charity, {Charity_Name}.

9. Generate a volunteer sign-up sheet for my community event, {Event_Name}.

10. Can you make a booking request form for my hotel, {Hotel_Name}?
