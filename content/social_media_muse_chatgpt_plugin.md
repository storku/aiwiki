## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. I'm posting a photo of my latest hiking adventure. What's a fitting soundtrack and hashtag for this exhilarating experience?

2. I've just taken a picture of my delicious homemade lasagna. What's the perfect song to go with this culinary masterpiece and a catchy hashtag?

3. Help me find a great soundtrack and hashtag for a breathtaking sunset photo I took at the beach.

4. I captured a photo of my adorable puppy taking a nap. What soundtrack and hashtag would best convey the cuteness overload?

5. What would be a fitting song choice and hashtag for a picture from my last-minute road trip with friends?

6. I need a soundtrack and hashtag suggestion to pair with the photo of my new workout gear for my fitness journey.

7. Help me pick a soundtrack and hashtag for a picture of my cozy reading nook and the book I'm currently reading.

8. I took an amazing photo of a city skyline at night. What would be a suitable song and hashtag to compliment the urban vibe?

9. What soundtrack and hashtag should I choose to go with the photo of my recent art project – a colorful mosaic?

10. I captured a candid moment of my family enjoying a backyard BBQ. What's the perfect soundtrack and hashtag to give off those warm, happy vibes?
