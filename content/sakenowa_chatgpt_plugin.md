## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Find me Sake brands with the flavor tag '甘口'.

2. Which Sake brands have a flavor profile of 'キレ味'?

3. Please recommend Sake brands with the flavor tag '酸味'.

4. I would like information on Sake brands from the area with prefecture number {prefecture_number} known for high-quality products.

5. Can you suggest some Sake brands with a '濃厚' flavor profile?

6. Provide details on award-winning Sake brands from the area with prefecture number {prefecture_number}.

7. I'm looking for Sake brands with a flavor profile similar to {brand_name}, can you help?

8. What are the top-rated Sake brands with a 'バランス良' flavor profile?

9. Please find me a highly recommended Sake brand with the flavor tag '複雑味'.

10. Which Sake brands from the area with prefecture number {prefecture_number} have a flavor profile of '低アルコール'?
