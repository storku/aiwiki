## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Can you suggest some budget hotels in {city} that are close to public transportation for my trip next month?

2. I'm looking for eco-friendly accommodations in {destination} for my sustainable travel experience. Any recommendations?

3. What are some boutique hotels in {city} that offer a unique and personalized experience?

4. Find me a camping site or RV park near {city} for my upcoming outdoor adventure weekend.

5. Show me wheelchair-accessible hotels in {city} with in-room accessibility features.

6. I am attending a conference at {venue} in {city}. Can you recommend hotels within walking distance?

7. Find me a hotel in {city} with a rooftop bar or restaurant for an extraordinary dining experience during my vacation next month.

8. What are the top-rated family-friendly activities and attractions in {destination}?

9. Suggest some luxury hotels in {city} with an in-hotel wine tasting experience for my weekend getaway.

10. I'm looking for an all-inclusive resort in {destination} with water sports and entertainment options for my vacation in {season}.
