## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Find the lowest price available for product_name in my area.

2. Show me a list of related products to this one: insert_product_url_here.

3. Provide me with the sales and discounts happening right now for product_name.

4. I want to compare the prices of product_name at different stores.

5. Help me find updated specifications and features of this product: product_id.

6. Find me the highest rated and lowest priced product similar to this one: product_id.

7. I'm looking to buy an eco-friendly version of product_name. Can you recommend one?

8. What are the shipping charges for this product if I order it online: product_id?

9. Provide me with a price comparison between brands for product_name.

10. Find me a better deal on this product: insert_image_url_here.
