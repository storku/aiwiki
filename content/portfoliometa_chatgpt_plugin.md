## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Compare the performance of [stock_ticker_1] and [stock_ticker_2].

2. What is the historical price data for [your_stock_ticker]?

3. Which upcoming events could impact [your_stock_ticker]?

4. Provide a technical analysis for [your_stock_ticker].

5. What are the latest analyst recommendations for [your_stock_ticker]?

6. Find the dividend history for [your_stock_ticker].

7. List the top institutional investors of [your_stock_ticker].

8. What is the market sentiment towards [your_stock_ticker]?

9. Retrieve the latest significant insider transactions for [your_stock_ticker].

10. Provide a summary of recent news articles related to [your_stock_ticker].
