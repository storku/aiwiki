## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. What are the recent advancements and discoveries in quantum computing research?

2. Can you provide me with the top 5 most cited Arxiv papers on artificial intelligence in the last two years?

3. What are the main topics currently being researched in the field of biotechnology?

4. Find research papers discussing the applications of machine learning in healthcare.

5. List three important research papers in the field of material science that have been published in the last year.

6. What are some promising new areas of research in renewable energy?

7. What are the recent developments in the usage of blockchain technology in supply chain management?

8. Provide me with a summary of the latest research on autonomous vehicle technology from Arxiv.

9. What are the current trends in edge computing research, and what are some notable papers on the topic?

10. What are the most popular research topics in data science and big data analytics in the last 3 years?
