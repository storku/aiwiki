**FiscalNote** is a [Plugin](/wiki/plugin) for [ChatGPT](/wiki/chatgpt).              

## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Provide me with a list of recent regulations related to {industry}.

2. What are the key political developments regarding {topic} in the last month?

3. Search for any statements made by {Senator's name} on {issue}.

4. What was the recent Congressional vote on {bill_name}?

5. Find all of President Biden's executive orders related to {topic}.

6. What is the latest update on the {legislation_name}?

7. Search for news about {Congressperson's name} involvement in {committee_name}.

8. List all the bills introduced by {Congressperson's name} in the current session.

9. What are the recent regulatory changes affecting the {sector_name} sector?

10. Who are the co-sponsors of the bill {bill_name}?
