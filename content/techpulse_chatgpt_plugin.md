## Guide

### Example Prompts

Example prompts that utilize the plugin:

1. Show me the top 5 stories on Hacker News related to Artificial Intelligence.

2. Fetch the latest cybersecurity news on Hacker News.

3. What are the top blockchain or cryptocurrency stories on Hacker News today?

4. Can you provide me with the most popular programming-related stories on Hacker News?

5. Find the latest 'Show HN' stories featuring Machine Learning or Data Science.

6. Show me the most upvoted job postings on Hacker News this week.

7. What are the top remote work opportunities listed on Hacker News?

8. Can you give me a summary of the current highest-rated discussions in 'Ask HN'?

9. Find me the most shared and engaging stories related to software development on Hacker News.

10. What are the trending topics in the 'Show HN' category today?
